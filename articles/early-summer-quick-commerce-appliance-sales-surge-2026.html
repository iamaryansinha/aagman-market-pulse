Early Summer Drives Quick Commerce, Appliance Sales Surge 15-20%

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Early summer drives 15-20% demand surge across quick commerce and e-commerce platforms for cooling essentials and appliances
  • 02/Flipkart reports 3X spike in swimming essentials, Amazon sees 50% YoY growth in premium AC sales as temperatures rise
  • 03/Industry executives expect further acceleration in coming weeks with lassi, ice cream and buttermilk projected to scale up 4X

An early onset of summer is triggering a sharp uptick in consumer demand across quick commerce and e-commerce platforms. Industry executives expect demand to accelerate 15-20% higher in the coming weeks as temperatures climb.

Flipkart is witnessing a 3X surge in swimming essentials and ice cube trays. The platform also reports a two-fold spike in men's caps as consumers rush to beat the heat.

Amazon India reports premium air conditioners have clocked over 50% year-on-year growth. Premium refrigerators are seeing strong double-digit expansion led by Ahmedabad, Mumbai and Delhi.

Quick commerce platforms including Blinkit, Zepto and Swiggy Instamart are experiencing unprecedented demand for summer essentials. Zepto saw beverage sales nearly double in April compared to January.

Ice cream demand jumped over 50% in March on Zepto. Mango ice cream, ice trays, and pre-frozen ice cubes are flying off virtual shelves across platforms.

Demand is spreading beyond metros into Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. AC demand doubled and refrigerator sales rose 1.5X in smaller cities as of April.

Flipkart Minutes reports health and energy drink sales tripled between March and April. Buttermilk and aerated beverages doubled as consumers seek cooling relief.

Industry executives expect lassi, ice cream and buttermilk demand to scale up by 4X. Ice cube sales could potentially grow by 6X as the summer intensifies.
