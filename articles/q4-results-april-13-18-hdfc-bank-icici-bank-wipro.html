Q4 Results Today & This Week: 42 Companies Including HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Wipro Reporting

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Six companies report Q4 results on April 13: ICICI Prudential AMC, Just Dial, Swaraj Engines, Innovision, Ind Bank Housing, Continental Controls
  • 02/42 companies scheduled this week including banking giants HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank on April 18, IT bellwether Wipro on April 16
  • 03/ICICI Pru AMC expected to post 5% net profit growth; markets open weak with Sensex down 1,620 points

The Q4 FY26 earnings season begins today with six companies announcing results. ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Ltd (NSE: ICICIPRULI), Just Dial Ltd (NSE: JUSTDIAL), Swaraj Engines Ltd (NSE: SWARAJENG), Innovision Ltd, Ind Bank Housing Ltd, and Continental Controls Ltd are all scheduled to report on April 13.

ICICI Prudential AMC shares traded at Rs 3,350.80 on the NSE, down 1% at 9.37 am. The stock opened at Rs 3,348.60 from the previous close of Rs 3,385.50. Analysts expect the company to report a 5 per cent increase in net profit for the March quarter.

The week of April 13-18 will see at least 42 listed companies announcing their financial results. The heaviest day is April 18 when private sector banking giants HDFC Bank Ltd (NSE: HDFCBANK) and ICICI Bank Ltd (NSE: ICICIBANK) release their audited standalone and consolidated earnings.

Both HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank boards are expected to consider dividend recommendations for FY26. YES Bank Ltd (NSE: YESBANK) will also report on April 18. HDFC Bank's results will be closely watched as they are the first since the resignation of part-time chairman Atanu Chakraborty in mid-March.

IT bellwether Wipro Ltd (NSE: WIPRO) will report on April 16. The board may also consider a share buyback proposal. Fellow April 16 reporters include HDFC Asset Management Company, HDFC Life Insurance, CRISIL Ltd, Angel One Ltd, and VST Industries Ltd.

April 15 brings 12 companies including HDB Financial Services, ICICI Lombard General Insurance, and Tejas Networks. IT services firm Mastek reports on April 17. The market opened weak today with BSE Sensex down 1,620.21 points at 75,930.04 and Nifty 50 down 461 points at 23,589.60.
