Auto Sales March 2026: Mahindra Leads With 25% Growth, Hyundai Hits Record

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Mahindra & Mahindra leads March auto sales with 25% YoY growth, dispatching 60,272 SUVs domestically
  • 02/Hyundai Motor India posts highest-ever March sales at 55,064 units, up 6.3% YoY
  • 03/FY2026 outlook remains cautious amid geopolitical uncertainties despite strong quarterly performance

Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd (NSE: M&M) reported domestic wholesales of 60,272 vehicles in March 2026. This marks a 25 per cent year-on-year growth compared to 48,048 units in March 2025.

The company's SUV sales hit an all-time high for any March. Total vehicle sales including LCVs reached 99,969 units, up 21 per cent YoY.

In the light commercial vehicles segment, M&M sold 24,928 units. This represents an 11 per cent increase over the 22,488 units sold in March 2025.

For the full financial year FY2025-26, Mahindra's domestic dispatches grew 20 per cent to 6,60,276 units. The LCV segment closed the year at 2,89,597 units, up 13 per cent YoY.

"The financial year ended on a very positive note, with Mahindra clocking its highest-ever volumes in both SUVs and LCVs segments," said Nalinikanth Gollagunta, CEO, Automotive Division, M&M.

Hyundai Motor India Ltd recorded domestic wholesales of 55,064 units in March. This is the company's highest-ever domestic sales for any March, with 6.3 per cent YoY growth.

The Korean automaker achieved its highest-ever quarterly domestic sales of 1,66,578 units in Q4 FY2025-26. "While we stay mindful of the prevailing geopolitical uncertainties, Hyundai Motor India is well-prepared for a strong FY2026-27," said Tarun Garg, Managing Director and CEO, HMIL.

JSW MG Motor India reported 19 per cent YoY growth with 6,528 wholesale units in March 2026. This compares to 5,500 units dispatched in the same month last year.
