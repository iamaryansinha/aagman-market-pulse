CoinDCX ₹100 Cr Digital Suraksha Fund to Combat Rising Cybercrime

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/CoinDCX commits ₹100 crore to Digital Suraksha Network for fighting cybercrime in India
  • 02/Cybercrime complaints hit 28.15 lakh in 2025, marking a sharp 24% spike year-over-year
  • 03/Initiative signals crypto exchange's push toward public safety beyond trading operations

CoinDCX has announced a ₹100 crore fund for the Digital Suraksha Network. The crypto exchange aims to combat rising cybercrime affecting Indian citizens.

The initiative comes as cybercrime reports surge nationwide. Ministry of Home Affairs data shows 28.15 lakh complaints filed in 2025. This marks a 24% increase over the previous year.

The Digital Suraksha Network focuses on cybersecurity awareness and protection. CoinDCX becomes one of the first crypto platforms to back such an initiative at this scale.

Cyber fraud has emerged as a major concern for digital asset users. Scams targeting crypto investors have multiplied as adoption grows across tier-2 and tier-3 cities.

The ₹100 crore commitment will likely fund education programs and victim support. It may also support technology solutions for detecting and preventing online fraud.

This move follows broader industry efforts to build trust. Crypto exchanges face regulatory pressure to demonstrate responsibility beyond compliance.
