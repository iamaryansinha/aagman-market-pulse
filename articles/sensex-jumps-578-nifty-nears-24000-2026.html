Sensex Jumps 578 Pts, Nifty Nears 24,000 on Strong Global Cues

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/BSE Sensex surged 578 points in early trade, crossing the 79,000 mark driven by positive global market sentiment
  • 02/Nifty 50 index approached the 24,000 level, gaining momentum from strong Asian and US market cues
  • 03/All sectoral indices traded in the green, with banking and IT stocks leading the rally

Indian equity markets opened sharply higher on April 10, 2026. The BSE Sensex jumped 578 points in early trade.

The 30-share benchmark index crossed the 79,000 mark. It was trading at 79,125, up 0.74% from the previous close.

The Nifty 50 index approached the key 24,000 level. It gained 168 points to trade at 23,987.

Global cues remained supportive for Indian markets. Asian markets traded higher following overnight gains on Wall Street.

The US Dow Jones Industrial Average closed 1.1% higher. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq also posted solid gains overnight.

All sectoral indices on the NSE traded in positive territory. Nifty Bank led the gains with a 1.2% surge.

IT stocks contributed significantly to the rally. Infosys and TCS gained over 1.5% each in early deals.

Banking heavyweights supported the index momentum. HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank rose 1.3% and 1.1% respectively.

Analysts expect the positive sentiment to continue. The broader market breadth remained favorable with more advancing stocks than decliners.

Midcap and smallcap indices also participated in the rally. The Nifty Midcap 100 gained 0.9% while Smallcap 100 rose 0.7%.
