Arvind SmartSpaces ESOP Trust Acquires 1% Stake via Secondary Market

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/ASL ESOP Trust purchased 4,58,670 equity shares representing nearly 1% stake from the secondary market on March 27, 2026
  • 02/The move comes shortly after promoters acquired ~4% stake from secondary market, increasing their shareholding from 49.83% to about 53.83%
  • 03/The trust route enables cashless ESOP exercise for employees while avoiding equity dilution for existing shareholders

Arvind SmartSpaces Limited (NSE: ARVSMART) has strengthened its ESOP framework through a trust-based structure. The ASL ESOP Trust acquired 4,58,670 equity shares from the open market on March 27, 2026.

The acquisition represents approximately 1% of the company's total equity. The transaction was executed through secondary market purchases at prevailing market prices.

The ESOP trust formation follows shareholder approval received earlier this month. The company implemented the 'Arvind SmartSpaces Limited – Employee Stock Option Scheme 2025' and the 'Arvind Infrastructure Limited Employee Stock Option Plan 2016' through an irrevocable employee welfare trust.

The ESOP-related move comes shortly after a promoter-led acquisition of around 4% stake from the secondary market. This increased promoter shareholding from 49.83% to approximately 53.83%.

The back-to-back transactions signal continued promoter confidence in the company's fundamentals. Both moves align with building a high-performance, ownership-driven organisation.

The trust route offers distinct advantages for employees and shareholders. Employees benefit from a cashless exercise mechanism without upfront payment of exercise price or taxes.

For shareholders, the secondary market acquisition eliminates the need for fresh equity issuance. This approach avoids dilution and any impact on earnings per share.

"The formalisation of our ESOP Trust is a key step in our evolution as a professionally managed, growth-focused organisation," stated Kulin Lalbhai, Chairman of Arvind SmartSpaces.
