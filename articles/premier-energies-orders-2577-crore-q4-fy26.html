Premier Energies Bags ₹2,577 Cr Solar Orders in Q4 FY26 for 1,600 MW

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Premier Energies wins ₹2,577 crore orders in Q4 FY26 to supply 1,600 MW of solar cells and modules to domestic clients
  • 02/Total order book reaches ₹13,723 crore covering 9.4 GW; provides revenue visibility through FY2028
  • 03/Cell capacity expanding to 10.6 GW by September 2026; module capacity at 11.1 GW

Premier Energies Limited (NSE: PREMIERENE) has bagged orders worth ₹2,577 crore in the fourth quarter of FY26. The contracts cover supply of 1,600 MW solar cells and modules.

Execution of these orders is scheduled across FY2027 and FY2028. The company disclosed this in a stock exchange filing on April 9, 2026.

The orders come from domestic Independent Power Producers (IPPs), module manufacturers, and EPC contractors. This diversified client base reduces dependency on any single segment.

Managing Director Chiranjeev Saluja said: "This robust order inflow underscores the trust placed by our customers in our manufacturing capabilities and technology roadmap."

The company now holds an order book of approximately ₹13,723 crore covering 9.4 GW. Cell orders contribute ₹6,800 crore while module orders account for ₹7,000 crore.

Premier Energies is scaling manufacturing capacity aggressively. Cell capacity is expected to reach 10.6 GW by September 2026. Module capacity has been expanded to 11.1 GW.

The Hyderabad-based company reported Q2 FY26 revenue of ₹1,836.87 crore, up 20.27% YoY. PAT jumped 71.61% to ₹353.44 crore.

Founded in 1995, Premier Energies manufactures solar PV cells and modules. It also provides EPC services for ground-mounted and rooftop solar projects.
