Arvind SmartSpaces ESOP Trust Acquires 1% Stake via Secondary Market

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Arvind SmartSpaces ESOP Trust acquired nearly 1% stake by purchasing 4,58,670 shares from the open market on March 27, 2026
  • 02/The acquisition follows shareholder approval for ASL Employee Stock Option Scheme 2025 enabling cashless exercise for employees
  • 03/Promoter shareholding increased from 49.83% to 53.83% recently, signaling strong confidence in company fundamentals

Arvind SmartSpaces Ltd (ASL) has strengthened its ESOP framework. The company's ESOP Trust acquired nearly 1% stake in the firm.

The Trust purchased 4,58,670 equity shares from the open market. The transaction was executed on March 27, 2026.

This move follows shareholder approval for the ASL Employee Stock Option Scheme 2025. The scheme introduces several key benefits for employees.

Kulin Lalbhai of Arvind SmartSpaces commented on the development. "Formalisation of ESOP Trust is key step in evolution," he stated.

The ESOP Trust structure enables cashless exercise for employees. Staff can exercise options without making upfront payments.

No fresh equity will be issued under this arrangement. Existing shareholders face zero dilution from the ESOP scheme.

The Trust also ensures smoother and faster settlement processes. This improves the overall employee experience significantly.

Promoters have also been actively acquiring shares from the secondary market. They recently purchased approximately 4% additional stake.

Promoter shareholding has increased from 49.83% to approximately 53.83%. This signals strong promoter confidence in the company's fundamentals.

Arvind SmartSpaces is a real estate development company. It focuses on residential and commercial projects across India.
