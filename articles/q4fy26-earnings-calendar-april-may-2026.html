Q4FY26 Earnings Calendar April-May 2026: TCS, Infosys, HDFC Bank Key Dates

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/TCS opens Q4FY26 earnings season on April 9; Infosys follows on April 23 with HDFC Bank on April 18
  • 02/Over 100 companies across IT, banking, FMCG and metals scheduled to report between April 7 and May 30
  • 03/Earnings season to provide cues on demand trends, margin pressures amid global uncertainties

The Q4FY26 earnings season kicks off this week with over 100 companies scheduled to report. The season begins on April 7 with Alka India and Ikoma Technologies leading the announcements.

Tata Consultancy Services (NSE: TCS) opens the Big Tech reporting season on April 9. The IT major's results will set the tone for the technology sector. Analysts expect commentary on US demand and margin trajectory.

April 18 features a banking double-header. HDFC Bank (NSE: HDFCBANK) and ICICI Bank (NSE: ICICIBANK) both report. Their results will offer insights into credit growth and asset quality trends.

Infosys (NSE: INFY) reports on April 23 alongside LTIMindtree. The week also sees Tata Capital and Adani Energy Solutions announcing numbers. Expect focus on IT deal pipelines and pricing pressure.

Wipro (NSE: WIPRO) reports on April 16 alongside HDFC Asset Management and HDFC Life. HCL Technologies (NSE: HCLTECH) and Nestle India follow on April 21. Tech Mahindra closes the IT major cycle on April 22.

May brings heavyweights from other sectors. Mahindra & Mahindra reports on May 5. JSW Steel on May 14 and Hindalco on May 22 will offer cues on commodity demand and pricing power.

The earnings season arrives amid elevated crude prices and geopolitical tensions. Management commentary on FY27 guidance will be closely watched by investors positioning for the new fiscal year.
