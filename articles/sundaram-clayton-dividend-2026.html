Sundaram Clayton Dividend 2026: Board Approves ₹4.50 Per Share Payout

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Sundaram-Clayton board approves interim dividend of ₹4.50 per share for FY2026, representing 90% on face value
  • 02/Record date fixed at March 27, 2026; same-day eligibility for shareholders holding equity shares
  • 03/Payout comes despite Q3 FY26 net loss of ₹51.92 crore; annual dividend yield stands at 0.37%

Sundaram-Clayton Limited (NSE: SUNCLAY, BSE: 544066) has declared an interim dividend of ₹4.50 per equity share. The board approved the payout at its meeting held on March 27, 2026.

The record date to determine eligible shareholders is March 27, 2026. Shares will trade ex-dividend from the same date.

The dividend represents 90% on the face value of ₹5 per share. The company has approximately 2.2 crore outstanding equity shares.

This marks a slight reduction from the previous interim dividend of ₹4.75 per share declared in March 2025. The total payout for FY2025 was ₹5.15 per share including final dividend.

Sundaram-Clayton reported a net loss of ₹51.92 crore in Q3 FY2026 on revenue of ₹506.05 crore. The dividend payout demonstrates commitment to shareholder returns despite weak quarterly earnings.

The company has declared dividends consistently over the past decade. However, the dividend yield has declined from historical peaks to current 0.37%.

The stock closed at ₹1,362 on March 26, 2026. Shares have declined 39% over the past year, underperforming broader auto component indices.

Sundaram-Clayton is part of the TVS Group led by Venu Srinivasan. The company manufactures aluminum die-castings and braking systems for automotive and industrial applications.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS THE INTERIM DIVIDEND DECLARED BY SUNDARAM CLAYTON FOR FY2026?
SUNDARAM-CLAYTON LIMITED HAS DECLARED AN INTERIM DIVIDEND OF ₹4.50 PER EQUITY SHARE FOR THE FINANCIAL YEAR ENDING MARCH 31, 2026. THIS REPRESENTS 90% DIVIDEND ON THE FACE VALUE OF ₹5 PER SHARE.
WHAT IS THE RECORD DATE FOR THE SUNDARAM CLAYTON DIVIDEND?
THE RECORD DATE TO DETERMINE ELIGIBILITY OF SHAREHOLDERS FOR THE INTERIM DIVIDEND IS FRIDAY, MARCH 27, 2026. THE EX-DIVIDEND DATE IS ALSO MARCH 27, 2026.
HOW DOES THIS DIVIDEND COMPARE TO PREVIOUS PAYOUTS?
THE ₹4.50 PER SHARE PAYOUT IS SLIGHTLY LOWER THAN THE ₹4.75 INTERIM DIVIDEND DECLARED IN MARCH 2025. FOR FY2025, THE TOTAL DIVIDEND PAYOUT WAS ₹5.15 PER SHARE INCLUDING THE FINAL DIVIDEND.
WHAT IS THE FINANCIAL POSITION OF SUNDARAM CLAYTON?
THE COMPANY REPORTED A NET LOSS OF ₹51.92 CRORE IN Q3 FY2026 ON REVENUE OF ₹506.05 CRORE. AS OF MARCH 15, 2026, TOTAL OUTSTANDING DEBT STOOD AT ₹121.7 CRORE INCLUDING FUND-BASED BORROWINGS OF ₹86.6 CRORE.
WHAT SHOULD INVESTORS DO TO RECEIVE THE DIVIDEND?
SHAREHOLDERS HOLDING SUNDARAM-CLAYTON SHARES IN THEIR DEMAT ACCOUNTS AS OF MARCH 27, 2026 WILL BE ENTITLED TO RECEIVE THE DIVIDEND. THE AMOUNT WILL BE CREDITED TO THEIR REGISTERED BANK ACCOUNTS WITHIN 30 DAYS OF THE RECORD DATE.
