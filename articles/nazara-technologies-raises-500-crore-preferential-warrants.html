Nazara Technologies Raises ₹500 Crore via Preferential Warrant Issue

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Nazara Technologies raises ₹500 crore through preferential issue of warrants to select investors
  • 02/Proceeds to fund strategic acquisitions including Bluetile and BestPlay transactions
  • 03/Company aims to accelerate growth across existing gaming and esports business verticals

Nazara Technologies Ltd (NSE: NAZARA) has raised ₹500 crore through a preferential issue of warrants. The company announced the fundraise on March 30, 2026.

The board approved the allotment of warrants to select investors. The funds will support strategic acquisitions and growth initiatives.

Primary use of proceeds includes the Bluetile acquisition. The BestPlay transaction will also receive funding from this raise.

Bluetile and BestPlay acquisitions align with Nazara's expansion strategy. The company continues building its gaming and esports portfolio.

Existing business verticals will see accelerated growth. Nazara operates across gaming, esports, and gamified early learning segments.

The preferential issue route allows targeted capital raising. Select investors participate at predetermined terms.

Nazara shares have shown volatility amid the gaming sector evolution. The fundraise strengthens the balance sheet for future deals.

THE PROCEEDS WILL BE USED TO SUPPORT STRATEGIC ACQUISITIONS INCLUDING THE BLUETILE AND BESTPLAY TRANSACTIONS, AND TO ACCELERATE GROWTH ACROSS EXISTING BUSINESS VERTICALS.
WHAT IS A PREFERENTIAL ISSUE OF WARRANTS?
A PREFERENTIAL ISSUE OF WARRANTS ALLOWS A COMPANY TO RAISE CAPITAL BY ISSUING FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS TO SELECT INVESTORS AT PREDETERMINED TERMS, GIVING THEM THE RIGHT TO SUBSCRIBE TO SHARES IN THE FUTURE.
WHAT ARE NAZARA TECHNOLOGIES' MAIN BUSINESS VERTICALS?
NAZARA OPERATES IN GAMING, ESPORTS, AND GAMIFIED EARLY LEARNING SEGMENTS. THE COMPANY IS ONE OF INDIA'S LEADING DIVERSIFIED GAMING AND ESPORTS PLATFORMS.
WHICH ACQUISITIONS IS NAZARA FUNDING WITH THIS RAISE?
NAZARA IS USING THE PROCEEDS TO FUND THE BLUETILE ACQUISITION AND THE BESTPLAY TRANSACTION, AMONG OTHER STRATEGIC INITIATIVES.
