Zomato Rebrands as Eternal: Company Name Changes, Food App Unchanged

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Zomato Limited has officially rebranded as Eternal Limited following board approval on February 6, 2025 and shareholder approval on March 10, 2025.
  • 02/The consumer-facing Zomato app will continue operating under its original name with no disruption to users.
  • 03/Stock ticker expected to change from ZOMATO to ETERNAL; corporate website shifts from zomato.com to eternal.com.

Zomato Limited has officially rebranded as Eternal Limited. The company announced the name change after receiving over 99% shareholder approval on March 10, 2025.

The board had approved the renaming on February 6, 2025. The change reflects Zomato's evolution beyond food delivery into a multi-business holding company.

The Zomato food delivery app will retain its name. Users will see no changes to the app interface or services.

Eternal now operates four distinct business verticals. These include Zomato (food delivery), Blinkit (quick commerce), Hyperpure (B2B supplies), and District (events and dining-out).

The stock ticker is expected to change from ZOMATO to ETERNAL. The corporate website has shifted from zomato.com to eternal.com.

Blinkit has become Eternal's largest revenue driver. The quick commerce unit now contributes over 70% of total revenue, surpassing the food delivery business.

Blinkit revenue surged 600% in 2025. The business grew from ₹1,399 crore in Q3 FY25 to ₹9,891 crore in Q2 FY26.

Founder Deepinder Goyal called the rebrand "a mission statement." He stated: "This isn't just a name change; it is a mission statement. A reminder etched into our identity that we will endure."

The company joined India's BSE Sensex benchmark index in December 2024. It currently commands a market capitalization of approximately $25-32 billion.
