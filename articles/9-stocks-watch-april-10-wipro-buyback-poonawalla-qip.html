Stocks to Watch April 10: Wipro Buyback, Poonawalla ₹2,500 Cr QIP

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 2 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Wipro board will consider share buyback proposal at April 16 meeting alongside Q4 FY26 results announcement
  • 02/Poonawalla Fincorp board approves QIP to raise ₹2,500 crore with floor price at ₹370.75 per share
  • 03/Prestige Estates acquires remaining 50% in Aaramnagar Realty for ₹180 crore; project saleable area 20 lakh sq ft valued at ₹1,800 crore

Stock markets are set for stock-specific action on Friday with nine companies making key corporate announcements. Wipro, Poonawalla Fincorp, and Prestige Estates Projects lead the list of stocks in focus.

Wipro (NSE: WIPRO) announced its board will consider a buyback proposal on April 16, 2026. The meeting was originally scheduled to announce Q4 and FY26 results. The buyback agenda has been added to the board meeting.

Poonawalla Fincorp (NSE: POONAWALLA) approved opening a Qualified Institutional Placement to raise approximately ₹2,500 crore. The board has fixed an indicative floor price of ₹370.75 per share. The company retains a green shoe option for oversubscription.

Prestige Estates Projects (NSE: PRESTIGE) acquired the remaining 50% stake in Aaramnagar Realty LLP. The transaction closed on April 9, 2026, for ₹180 crore in capital and current account contributions.

Aaramnagar Realty was formed in 2018 and recorded no turnover in the past three years. The acquisition enables Prestige to develop a senior living residential community in Versova, Mumbai.

The project spans approximately 20 lakh square feet with projected sales value of ₹1,800 crore. Prestige described this as its largest land acquisition to date.

Steel Exchange India has repaid ₹28 crore over the past two quarters. The company repaid ₹21.43 crore toward term loans and ₹7.09 crore toward NCDs between October 2025 and March 2026.

GHV Infra Projects secured a ₹111 crore work order from GHV (India). The project involves expanding the Carshed capacity at Sanpada with a 24-month completion timeline.

Amagi Media Labs expanded its Amagi NOW platform with Agentic Media Operations. The integration uses reasoning agents powered by Amagi Intelligence.

Yashtej Industries completed expanding its Soya Bean Solvent Extraction Plant capacity from 300 TCD to 500 TCD. Commercial operations began on April 9, 2026.

Emerald Finance partnered with Ebix Corporate Services to launch an Early-Wage-Access program for employees.

Ashiana Housing (NSE: ASHIANA) will also be in focus following strong Q4 pre-sales growth reported separately.
