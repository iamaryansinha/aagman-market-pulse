Rustomjee Record Q4 Pre-Sales: ₹1,346 Crore, Beats FY26 Guidance

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Keystone Realtors reports highest-ever quarterly pre-sales of ₹13.46 billion in Q4FY26, marking 58% YoY growth
  • 02/Full-year pre-sales of ₹40.22 billion exceeded guidance, up 33% from FY25's ₹30.28 billion
  • 03/Company added 5 new projects worth ₹104.20 billion GDV; watch FY27 launch pipeline and redevelopment focus

Keystone Realtors Ltd (NSE: KEYSTONE), operating under the Rustomjee brand, has reported record quarterly pre-sales of ₹13.46 billion for Q4FY26. The figure represents a sharp 58% jump from ₹8.54 billion in Q4FY25.

The Mumbai-based developer achieved its full-year pre-sales guidance of ₹40.22 billion for FY26. This marks a 33% increase over FY25's ₹30.28 billion.

Collections remained robust at ₹8.53 billion in Q4FY26, up 14% YoY from ₹7.47 billion. For the full year, collections totaled ₹26.21 billion compared to ₹23.27 billion in FY25.

The company launched two projects in Q4 with combined saleable area of 0.69 million sq ft. The estimated gross development value of these launches stood at ₹39.78 billion.

For FY26, Keystone launched seven projects with total GDV of ₹98.13 billion. This is nearly double the ₹50.19 billion launched in FY25.

The company added five new projects during the year with GDV of ₹104.20 billion, marking 118% growth over previous year. These additions were largely redevelopment-led.

Operational area expanded to 2.12 million sq ft in FY26 from 1.69 million sq ft in FY25, reflecting 25% growth.

The company holds an "A+" credit rating with stable and positive outlook from rating agencies. Management confirmed performance exceeded guidance across all key metrics.

Mumbai's real estate market continues to show strong demand for quality housing. Keystone's redevelopment-focused strategy positions it well in this supply-constrained market.

The company completed six projects during FY26 with total construction area of 2.23 million sq ft. One new project was added in Q4 alone.
