Silver Price Today March 30: ₹250/Gram Across Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Silver trades at ₹250 per gram across all major Indian cities on March 30, 2026, unchanged from previous session
  • 02/One kilogram of silver costs ₹2,50,000 in Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Kolkata, Bengaluru and Ahmedabad
  • 03/Prices expected to remain stable; investors should watch global cues and USD-INR movement

Silver prices in India held steady at ₹250 per gram on March 30, 2026. One kilogram of the white metal is priced at ₹2,50,000 across major cities.

The rates remained unchanged from the previous trading session. This marks a day of consolidation in the domestic silver market.

Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Kolkata, Bengaluru and Ahmedabad all reported identical rates. Each city listed silver at ₹250 per gram and ₹2,50,000 per kg.

The price uniformity reflects stable wholesale market conditions. Domestic prices typically track international silver benchmarks and currency movements.

Investors use silver as both an industrial metal and a hedge against inflation. Jewellery and industrial demand drive consumption in India.

Global silver prices and the rupee-dollar exchange rate will influence future direction. Any significant movement in either could trigger price adjustments.
