Kia India Sales Jump 14.5% to 29,112 Units in March 2026, Sets Quarterly Record

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Kia India sold 29,112 units in March 2026, marking a 14.5% year-on-year growth
  • 02/Q1 2026 sales reached 84,316 units, up 11.6% from 75,576 units in Q1 2025
  • 03/This marks Kia India's strongest-ever quarterly performance since entering the market

Kia India has reported strong sales growth for March 2026. The company sold 29,112 units during the month.

This represents a 14.5% increase compared to March 2025. The momentum reflects sustained consumer demand for Kia's vehicle lineup.

The first quarter of 2026 has been exceptional for the Korean automaker. Kia delivered its strongest-ever quarterly performance.

Q1 sales reached 84,316 units. This is an 11.6% growth over the 75,576 units sold in Q1 2025.

Kia India operates in the competitive mass-market segment. It competes with Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, and Tata Motors.

The company's portfolio includes popular models like Seltos, Sonet, and Carens. These vehicles have helped Kia gain significant market share since its India entry in 2019.

Consistent double-digit growth indicates strong brand resonance. It also points to effective distribution and marketing strategies.

The Indian automotive market has shown recovery in 2026. Kia appears well-positioned to capitalize on this demand.

Investors tracking the auto sector should monitor April sales trends. The upcoming festive season will be critical for full-year performance.
