BuzzBallz Clocks 3x Monthly Growth in Bengaluru, Eyes Maharashtra and Goa Expansion

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/BuzzBallz has achieved 3x monthly growth in Bengaluru since market entry
  • 02/Captured 20% market share in RTD segment; present in 30% of city's RTD outlets
  • 03/Company plans expansion to Maharashtra and Goa markets next

BuzzBallz has reported 3x monthly growth in Bengaluru. The ready-to-drink cocktail brand has gained significant traction in the city.

The company has captured 20% market share in Bengaluru's RTD segment. This comes within months of entering the market.

Distribution now covers 30% of Bengaluru's RTD outlet universe. The brand is available across retail stores, bars, and restaurants.

Bengaluru was chosen as the India launch market due to its young demographic. The city's cosmopolitan culture aligns with the brand's positioning.

BuzzBallz plans to expand to Maharashtra and Goa next. These are high-consumption markets for alcoholic beverages.

The US-based brand entered India through a partnership with domestic distributors. It offers pre-mixed cocktails in portable pouches.

India's RTD alcohol market is growing at 25% annually. Premiumization trends are driving demand for convenience-oriented products.
