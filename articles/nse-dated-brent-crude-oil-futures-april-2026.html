NSE to Launch Dated Brent Crude Oil Futures From April 13, 2026

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/NSE will launch Dated Brent Crude Oil futures contracts on April 13, 2026, after receiving SEBI approval
  • 02/Contracts will be cash-settled in INR based on monthly average of Platts Dated Brent assessments
  • 03/NSE targets MCX's 85-90% market share in India's energy derivatives segment

The National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) will introduce Dated Brent Crude Oil futures contracts from April 13, 2026. The launch follows approval from the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI).

The contracts will be listed on a monthly basis. Trading hours are scheduled from Monday to Friday between 9:00 AM and 11:30 PM or 11:55 PM IST.

Settlement will be cash-based in Indian Rupees. The final settlement price is calculated using the monthly average of Platts Dated Brent assessments, converted to INR using RBI's reference rate.

Dated Brent is a leading international crude oil benchmark. It is used to price two-thirds of the world's internationally traded crude oil supplies.

The launch marks NSE's strategic push into energy derivatives. NSE entered commodity derivatives in October 2018, initially focusing on bullion before adding WTI Crude Oil and Natural Gas.

NSE is targeting the Multi Commodity Exchange of India's (MCX) dominance. MCX currently holds an estimated 85-90% market share in non-agricultural commodity derivatives.

Energy commodities account for nearly 70% of MCX's options trading volume. NSE aims to attract traders through advanced technology and margin fungibility across asset classes.

Global Brent crude prices have seen significant volatility. Prices exceeded $112 per barrel in late March 2026 due to geopolitical tensions.

The new contracts provide Indian market participants with direct access to global oil price discovery. This enables better hedging against crude oil price fluctuations.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS THE LAUNCH DATE FOR NSE'S DATED BRENT CRUDE OIL FUTURES?
THE NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA WILL LAUNCH DATED BRENT CRUDE OIL FUTURES CONTRACTS ON APRIL 13, 2026. THE LAUNCH HAS RECEIVED APPROVAL FROM THE SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA (SEBI).
HOW WILL THE BRENT CRUDE FUTURES CONTRACTS BE SETTLED?
THE CONTRACTS WILL BE CASH-SETTLED IN INDIAN RUPEES. THE FINAL SETTLEMENT PRICE IS BASED ON THE MONTHLY AVERAGE OF PLATTS DATED BRENT ASSESSMENTS, CONVERTED TO INR USING THE RESERVE BANK OF INDIA'S REFERENCE RATE.
WHAT ARE THE TRADING HOURS FOR THESE FUTURES CONTRACTS?
THE CONTRACTS WILL TRADE FROM MONDAY TO FRIDAY BETWEEN 9:00 AM AND 11:30 PM OR 11:55 PM INDIAN STANDARD TIME. MONTHLY CONTRACTS WILL BE LISTED FOR TRADING.
WHY IS NSE LAUNCHING BRENT CRUDE OIL FUTURES NOW?
NSE AIMS TO CHALLENGE MCX'S 85-90% MARKET SHARE IN ENERGY DERIVATIVES. THE EXCHANGE HAS BEEN EXPANDING ITS COMMODITY SEGMENT SINCE 2018 AND OFFERS MARGIN FUNGIBILITY ACROSS EQUITY AND COMMODITY SEGMENTS.
WHAT IS DATED BRENT AND WHY DOES IT MATTER?
DATED BRENT IS A LEADING INTERNATIONAL CRUDE OIL BENCHMARK USED TO PRICE TWO-THIRDS OF THE WORLD'S INTERNATIONALLY TRADED CRUDE OIL. IT ENABLES INDIAN MARKET PARTICIPANTS TO HEDGE AGAINST GLOBAL OIL PRICE VOLATILITY AND ACCESS INTERNATIONAL PRICE DISCOVERY.
