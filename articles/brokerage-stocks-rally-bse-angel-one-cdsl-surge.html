Brokerage Stocks Rally: BSE, Angel One, CDSL Surge Over 7% On Market Optimism

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Brokerage and capital market-linked stocks surge over 8% intraday, led by BSE Ltd and Angel One
  • 02/Motilal Oswal Financial Services rises 8.5% to ₹687.05, while BSE Ltd jumps 7% to ₹2,879.10
  • 03/Rally reflects strong investor sentiment toward capital market infrastructure stocks

Brokerage and capital market-linked stocks surged over 8% on April 1, 2026. BSE Ltd, Angel One, and CDSL led the rally.

Motilal Oswal Financial Services jumped 8.5% to ₹687.05. The stock gained sharply amid positive sector sentiment.

BSE Ltd climbed over 7% to ₹2,879.10. The exchange operator continues to attract strong investor interest.

The rally reflects optimism in India's capital markets ecosystem. Increased retail participation and trading volumes are driving broker profitability.

Central Depository Services (CDSL) and Angel One also featured among the top gainers. These stocks benefit from rising demat account openings.

The sector has shown resilience despite market volatility. Investors are betting on structural growth in India's financial markets.
