Citius TransNet Investment Trust IPO: ₹1,105-Crore Issue Opens April 17

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Citius TransNet Investment Trust's ₹1,105 crore IPO opens on April 17, 2026, with price band of ₹99-100 per unit
  • 02/The infrastructure investment trust will use ₹1,000 crore to acquire road assets including SRPL and project SPVs
  • 03/IPO closes April 21, allotment on April 24, and units will list on stock exchanges on April 29

Citius TransNet Investment Trust opens its ₹1,105 crore initial public offering on April 17, 2026. The price band has been fixed at ₹99-100 per unit.

The IPO will remain open for subscription until April 21. Anchor investor allocation was completed ahead of the public issue opening.

The infrastructure investment trust is sponsored by EAAA TransInfra Managers. It focuses on acquiring and managing transport infrastructure assets across India.

The trust will deploy ₹1,000 crore from the IPO proceeds. These funds will be used for partial or full acquisition of securities of SRPL (holding company).

The acquisition targets include project SPVs: Thrissur Expressway, Jorabat Shillong Expressway, Dhola Infra Projects, and Dibang Infra Projects. These are road infrastructure assets.

The remaining proceeds will be utilized for general corporate purposes. This includes meeting offer expenses and working capital requirements.

Investors can bid for a minimum of 150 units. Bids must be in multiples of 150 units thereafter.

The basis of allotment will be finalized on April 24, 2026. Successful allottees will receive units in their demat accounts by April 28.

Units of Citius TransNet Investment Trust will be available for trading on April 29. The units will list on both BSE and NSE.

The trust structure allows investors to participate in infrastructure assets. This provides exposure to annuity-based revenue models from road projects.
