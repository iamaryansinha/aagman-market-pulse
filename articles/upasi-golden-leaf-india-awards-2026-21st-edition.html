UPASI Golden Leaf India Awards 2026: 21st Edition Announced for South Indian Tea

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/UPASI will organize the 21st edition of Golden Leaf India Awards, marking two decades of promoting South Indian tea excellence
  • 02/The annual competition has significantly showcased the region's quality teas on the global stage since inception
  • 03/Winners will gain international visibility; awards serve as benchmark for tea quality standards in South India

The United Planters' Association of Southern India (UPASI) will organize the 21st edition of the Golden Leaf India Awards. The announcement was made on March 30, 2026.

The annual competition is highly anticipated by the South Indian tea community. It has played a pivotal role in promoting the region's quality teas globally over the past two decades.

The awards recognize excellence in tea production across multiple categories. Winners gain international visibility and credibility in export markets.

South Indian tea accounts for approximately 20% of India's total tea production. The region is known for its distinctive flavor profiles and orthodox tea varieties.

The Golden Leaf India Awards have become a benchmark for quality standards. They help tea estates differentiate their products in competitive global markets.

Exporters view the awards as a marketing tool. Winning entries often command premium prices in international auctions.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS THE GOLDEN LEAF INDIA AWARDS?
THE GOLDEN LEAF INDIA AWARDS IS AN ANNUAL COMPETITION ORGANIZED BY UPASI TO RECOGNIZE EXCELLENCE IN SOUTH INDIAN TEA PRODUCTION. THE 21ST EDITION HAS BEEN ANNOUNCED FOR 2026.
WHO ORGANIZES THE GOLDEN LEAF INDIA AWARDS?
THE UNITED PLANTERS' ASSOCIATION OF SOUTHERN INDIA (UPASI) ORGANIZES THE AWARDS. UPASI IS THE PREMIER PLANTERS' ASSOCIATION REPRESENTING TEA, COFFEE, AND RUBBER GROWERS IN SOUTH INDIA.
WHY ARE THESE AWARDS IMPORTANT FOR THE TEA INDUSTRY?
THE AWARDS HAVE SHOWCASED SOUTH INDIAN QUALITY TEAS GLOBALLY FOR TWO DECADES. THEY PROVIDE INTERNATIONAL VISIBILITY AND HELP TEA ESTATES COMMAND PREMIUM PRICES IN EXPORT MARKETS.
WHAT PERCENTAGE OF INDIA'S TEA PRODUCTION COMES FROM SOUTH INDIA?
SOUTH INDIAN TEA ACCOUNTS FOR APPROXIMATELY 20% OF INDIA'S TOTAL TEA PRODUCTION. THE REGION IS KNOWN FOR DISTINCTIVE FLAVOR PROFILES AND ORTHODOX TEA VARIETIES.
WHAT BENEFITS DO WINNERS RECEIVE?
WINNERS GAIN INTERNATIONAL VISIBILITY, CREDIBILITY IN EXPORT MARKETS, AND CAN USE THE AWARD AS A MARKETING TOOL. WINNING ENTRIES OFTEN COMMAND PREMIUM PRICES IN INTERNATIONAL AUCTIONS.
