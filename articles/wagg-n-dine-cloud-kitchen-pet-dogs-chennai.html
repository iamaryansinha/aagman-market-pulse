Wagg n Dine Launches Cloud Kitchen for Pet Dogs in Chennai

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Wagg n Dine launches cloud kitchen in Chennai offering freshly cooked, human-grade meals delivered to pet owners' doorsteps
  • 02/India's pet care industry growing at 20%+ annually with premium pet food segment expanding at over 20%
  • 03/Company targets urban pet parents through subscription model with customizable recurring deliveries

Wagg n Dine has launched a cloud kitchen in Chennai focused on pet nutrition. The startup offers freshly cooked, human-grade meals for dogs delivered directly to customers.

The company prepares meals daily using fresh chicken, clean RO water, and carefully sourced ingredients. It positions itself as an alternative to conventional processed pet food.

Founder Manivannan stated the pet care landscape is shifting from "feeding pets" to "nourishing family members". This reflects growing humanisation trends in the pet industry.

The pet care industry in India is growing at over 20% annually. The premium pet food segment is expanding at over 20% according to market data.

The pet food market overall is projected to grow at 6-8% annually. Fresh and human-grade nutrition represents the fastest-growing consumer segment.

Wagg n Dine launched a new website as the digital hub for its subscription service. Pet parents can customize and manage recurring deliveries tailored to their dog's nutritional needs.

Before public launch, the company conducted trials with a test group of pet owners. The response from both dogs and owners was positive, helping refine recipes and processes.

The company believes cloud kitchens for pets will drive the next growth wave. It targets convenience, customization, and clean-label meals for urban pet parents in cities like Chennai.
