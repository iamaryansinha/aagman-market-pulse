India Pushes PNG Expansion To 30 Lakh Connections Amid West Asia Crisis

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/India targets 30 lakh new piped natural gas connections across 150 high-priority districts amid West Asia supply disruptions
  • 02/60 lakh LPG connections exist near PNG networks and can migrate to piped gas for energy security
  • 03/National PNG Drive 2.0 extended till June 30, 2026 with three-week ward-wise action plans

India is accelerating piped natural gas expansion targeting 30 lakh connections across 150 districts. The push comes as West Asia conflict disrupts LPG supplies through the Strait of Hormuz.

The Ministries of Petroleum and Housing & Urban Affairs held a joint review meeting on Thursday. Oil Secretary Neeraj Mittal and MoHUA Secretary Srinivas Katikithala chaired the session with state officials.

The meeting focused on 110 identified geographic areas for PNG infrastructure expansion. A saturation approach will cover major cities including Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Pune, Ahmedabad and others.

Over 60 lakh LPG connections exist near operational PNG networks. These households can easily migrate to piped natural gas for cooking and heating needs.

More than 3.1 lakh connections were gasified in March 2026. Another 2.7 lakh new connections were issued during the same period.

All states and urban local bodies must establish single-window approval mechanisms. The Natural Gas & Petroleum Product Distribution Order 2026 supersedes all previous notifications.

A dedicated MIS portal will enable daily monitoring of PNG expansion progress. City gas distributors will map networks on the PM Gati Shakti portal for coordinated planning.

Three-week time-bound action plans with ward-wise targets are mandatory. Priority areas include locations with existing pipeline infrastructure and group housing societies.

Industrial users currently receive about 80 percent of average natural gas consumption. Full allocations are maintained for PNG and CNG segments.

HOW MANY PNG CONNECTIONS IS INDIA TARGETING AMID THE WEST ASIA CRISIS?
INDIA IS TARGETING 30 LAKH NEW PIPED NATURAL GAS CONNECTIONS ACROSS 150 HIGH-PRIORITY DISTRICTS. THE EXPANSION COVERS 110 IDENTIFIED GEOGRAPHIC AREAS WITH FOCUS ON URBAN HOUSEHOLDS.
WHY IS INDIA EXPANDING PNG NETWORKS NOW?
THE WEST ASIA CONFLICT HAS DISRUPTED LPG SUPPLIES THROUGH THE STRAIT OF HORMUZ. ABOUT 60 LAKH LPG CONNECTIONS EXIST NEAR PNG NETWORKS AND CAN MIGRATE TO PIPED GAS FOR ENERGY SECURITY.
WHICH CITIES ARE PART OF THE SATURATION APPROACH?
THE SATURATION APPROACH COVERS AHMEDABAD, BENGALURU, DELHI-NCR, LUCKNOW, MUMBAI, THANE, NAVI MUMBAI, PUNE, SURAT AND VARANASI TO ACHIEVE NEAR-UNIVERSAL PNG COVERAGE.
WHAT IS THE TIMELINE FOR THE PNG EXPANSION DRIVE?
THE NATIONAL PNG DRIVE 2.0 HAS BEEN EXTENDED TILL JUNE 30, 2026. DISTRICTS AND URBAN LOCAL BODIES MUST PREPARE THREE-WEEK TIME-BOUND ACTION PLANS WITH WARD-WISE TARGETS.
HOW WILL THE GOVERNMENT MONITOR PNG EXPANSION PROGRESS?
A DEDICATED MIS PORTAL WILL BE DEVELOPED FOR DAILY MONITORING. CITY GAS DISTRIBUTORS MUST MAP PIPELINE NETWORKS ON THE PM GATI SHAKTI NATIONAL MASTER PLAN PORTAL FOR TRANSPARENT TRACKING.
