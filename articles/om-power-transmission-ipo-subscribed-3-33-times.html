Om Power Transmission IPO Subscribed 3.33 Times on Final Day

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Om Power Transmission Ltd's Rs 150 crore IPO closed on April 13 with 3.33 times overall subscription
  • 02/Non-institutional investors led demand at 7.06x, followed by QIBs at 3.65x and retail at 1.54x
  • 03/The company will use fresh issue proceeds for capex, debt repayment, and working capital requirements

Om Power Transmission Ltd's initial public offering closed on April 13, 2026. The Rs 150 crore issue received bids for 1,99,65,140 shares against 60,02,730 shares on offer.

The IPO was subscribed 3.33 times overall as per NSE data at 17:30 IST. The price band was fixed at Rs 166-175 per share.

Non-institutional investors led the demand with 7.06 times subscription. Qualified institutional buyers subscribed their quota 3.65 times.

Retail individual investors subscribed their portion 1.54 times. The minimum lot size was 85 shares requiring Rs 14,875.

The issue comprised a fresh issue of 75.75 lakh shares and an offer for sale of 10 lakh shares. Promoters Kalpesh Patel, Kanubhai Patel, and Vasantkumar Patel sold the OFS portion.

Ahead of the IPO, the company raised Rs 45.01 crore from four anchor investors. It allotted 25.72 lakh shares at Rs 175 each on March 23, 2026.

Om Power Transmission is a Gujarat-based EPC company in power transmission. It primarily serves PSU clients accounting for approximately 84% of revenue.

For the nine months ended December 31, 2025, the company reported sales of Rs 274.54 crore. Net profit stood at Rs 23.37 crore during the same period.

The company plans to deploy Rs 11.21 crore towards capital expenditure. Another Rs 25 crore will go towards debt repayment and Rs 55 crore for working capital.
