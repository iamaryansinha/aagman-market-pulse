Karur Vysya Bank Stock Target: Analysts See 27% Upside to ₹375

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Karur Vysya Bank receives buy recommendation with target price of ₹375 from current levels near ₹296
  • 02/The stock offers 27% upside potential over the medium-term according to analyst estimates
  • 03/Current trading price is ₹295.85; investors should watch for momentum above key resistance levels

Karur Vysya Bank has received a buy recommendation from market analysts. The stock is currently trading at ₹295.85.

The target price is set at ₹375 for the medium-term. This implies an upside potential of approximately 27% from current levels.

Karur Vysya Bank is a south India-based private sector lender. It has a strong presence in Tamil Nadu and surrounding regions.

The bank has been focusing on retail loan growth and asset quality improvement. Its CASA ratio remains competitive among regional peers.

The stock has shown resilience in recent market conditions. It has outperformed several mid-cap banking stocks over the past quarter.

Investors should monitor quarterly earnings for loan growth trends. Asset quality metrics will be critical for the stock's movement toward the target.

The ₹375 target assumes continued operational performance and stable credit costs. Any deterioration in asset quality could impact projections.

Risk-averse investors may consider accumulating on dips. The stock offers a favorable risk-reward at current valuations.
