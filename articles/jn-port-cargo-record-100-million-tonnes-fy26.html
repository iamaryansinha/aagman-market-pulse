JN Port Cargo Hits Record 100 Million Tonnes in FY26, Container Traffic Surges 12.6%

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Jawaharlal Nehru Port Authority crosses historic 100 million tonnes cargo handling milestone in FY2026
  • 02/Container throughput reaches 7.94 million TEUs in CY2025, marking 12.64% year-on-year growth
  • 03/Port records highest monthly cargo throughput of 9.06 million tonnes in January 2026

Jawaharlal Nehru Port Authority (JNPA) has achieved a historic milestone by surpassing 100 million tonnes of cargo handling in FY2026. The port, India's largest container gateway, recorded total cargo volume of 99.17 million metric tonnes in calendar year 2025.

Container throughput reached a record 7.94 million twenty-foot equivalent units (TEUs) during CY2025. This represents a robust 12.64% increase compared to 7.05 million TEUs handled in the previous year.

The port achieved its highest-ever monthly container throughput of 7,37,682 TEUs in January 2026. This surpassed the previous record of 7,11,412 TEUs set in December 2025.

Total cargo throughput for January 2026 hit 9.06 million metric tonnes. This exceeded the earlier peak of 8.96 million metric tonnes recorded in December 2025.

JNPA primarily handles container cargo through five terminals: NSFT, NSICT, NSIGT, BMCT, and APMT. The port also manages liquid cargo terminals operated by BPCL-IOCL consortium and JSW-JNPT Liquid Terminal.

In H1 FY2026, JNPT handled 49.63 million tonnes of cargo. This positioned it as the third-largest port by volume among India's 12 major ports during the period.

Gaurav Dayal, IAS, Chairperson of JNPA, attributed the success to improved terminal efficiencies and better alignment of support services with rising demand. He noted the seamless coordination among terminal operators and logistics partners.

The port's performance reflects India's resilient trade sector despite global uncertainties. Steady growth in both exports and imports contributed to the higher cargo throughput during the fiscal year.

WHAT WAS JNPA'S CONTAINER THROUGHPUT IN CY2025?
JNPA HANDLED A RECORD 7.94 MILLION TEUS IN CALENDAR YEAR 2025, REPRESENTING A 12.64% INCREASE FROM 7.05 MILLION TEUS HANDLED IN CY2024.
WHAT CARGO TYPES DOES JNPA HANDLE?
JNPA PRIMARILY HANDLES CONTAINER CARGO THROUGH FIVE TERMINALS. THE PORT ALSO HANDLES LIQUID BULK INCLUDING POL (PETROLEUM, OIL, AND LUBRICANTS), DRY BULK, AND CEMENT THROUGH SPECIALIZED TERMINALS.
WHAT IS JNPA'S MONTHLY RECORD PERFORMANCE IN JANUARY 2026?
IN JANUARY 2026, JNPA ACHIEVED ITS HIGHEST-EVER MONTHLY CONTAINER THROUGHPUT OF 7,37,682 TEUS AND TOTAL CARGO THROUGHPUT OF 9.06 MILLION METRIC TONNES.
WHAT INFRASTRUCTURE DOES JNPA OPERATE TO HANDLE THIS VOLUME?
JNPA OPERATES FIVE CONTAINER TERMINALS (NSFT, NSICT, NSIGT, BMCT, APMT), A SHALLOW WATER BERTH FOR GENERAL CARGO, AND TWO LIQUID CARGO TERMINALS. THE PORT IS ALSO EXPANDING CAPACITY TO ACCOMMODATE LARGER VESSELS AND MEET GROWING DEMAND.
