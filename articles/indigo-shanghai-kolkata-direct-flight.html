IndiGo Launches Shanghai-Kolkata Direct Flight, Second India Route to China

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/IndiGo launches direct flight connecting Shanghai and Kolkata, the airline's second India-China route
  • 02/Kolkata becomes second Indian metro after New Delhi to have direct air link with Shanghai
  • 03/Route strengthens connectivity between India's eastern hub and China's commercial capital

IndiGo has launched a direct flight connecting Shanghai and Kolkata. The new route establishes direct air connectivity between China's commercial capital and India's eastern metropolis.

Kolkata becomes the second Indian metro after New Delhi to be directly linked with Shanghai. This expansion marks IndiGo's continued push into international markets.

The direct flight reduces travel time between the two cities. Passengers no longer need to transit through third-country hubs for this journey.

Shanghai serves as China's primary financial and commercial center. The route opens new opportunities for trade and business travel between eastern India and China.

IndiGo is India's largest airline by domestic market share. The carrier has been aggressively expanding its international network in recent years.

The Kolkata-Shanghai route complements IndiGo's existing India-China services. It provides travelers with more options for direct connectivity between the two countries.
