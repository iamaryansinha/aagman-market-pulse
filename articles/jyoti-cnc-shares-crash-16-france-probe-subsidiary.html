Jyoti CNC Shares Crash 16% as French Unit Faces Export Probe

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Jyoti CNC Automation shares plummeted 16% to ₹691.90 on April 13, 2026 after disclosure of French investigation into subsidiary
  • 02/French authorities seized EUR 4.0 million in bank accounts and two residential properties from Huron Graffenstaden SAS
  • 03/Probe centers on alleged export of dual-use technology machines violating EU export control laws

Shares of Jyoti CNC Automation Limited (NSE: JYOTICNC) crashed 16% on Monday. The stock tumbled to ₹691.90 from the previous close of ₹819.20.

The sharp decline followed a stock exchange disclosure late Sunday. French authorities have initiated a judicial probe into Huron Graffenstaden SAS, a material subsidiary.

The investigation relates to alleged export of machines with dual-use technology. French authorities suspect violations of European Union export control laws.

As interim measures, authorities restricted the subsidiary's director general from duties. Bank accounts totaling EUR 4.0 million have been seized.

Two residential properties owned by Jyoti SAS have also been attached. A formal judicial investigation has been initiated against the subsidiary and certain employees.

Huron Graffenstaden SAS has refuted the allegations. The subsidiary is seeking clarity from authorities and legal counsel to contest the charges strongly.

The company stated that business operations at the subsidiary continue as usual. Some assets and services of employees under investigation may be temporarily unavailable.

Jyoti CNC acquired a 70% stake in Huron Graffenstaden SAS in March 2023. The French unit specializes in high-precision machining centers.

The parent company emphasized that its standalone business remains unaffected. Jyoti CNC contributes over 85% of the Group's revenue from operations.

The company is awaiting formal outcomes while continuing to cooperate with French authorities. The investigation introduces significant regulatory and reputational risks for the stock.
