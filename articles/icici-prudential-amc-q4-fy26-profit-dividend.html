ICICI Prudential AMC Q4 FY26 Profit Up 10% To ₹763 Crore; ₹12.40 Dividend Declared

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Ltd reported Q4 FY26 net profit of ₹763 crore, up 10.37% YoY from ₹692 crore in Q4 FY25
  • 02/Revenue grew 19.5% to ₹1,517 crore while AUM surged 25% to ₹11.78 lakh crore on strong inflows
  • 03/The board recommended a final dividend of ₹12.40 per equity share for FY26, subject to shareholder approval

ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Ltd reported a 10.37% year-on-year rise in net profit to ₹763 crore for the quarter ended March 31, 2026. The asset manager had posted a profit of ₹692 crore in the same period last year.

Revenue for the quarter increased 19.5% to ₹1,517 crore against ₹1,269 crore in Q4 FY25. EBITDA climbed 29.7% to ₹1,160 crore from ₹894.1 crore in the year-ago period.

Operating margin improved to 76.47% in Q4 FY26 compared to 70.46% last year. Overall expenses declined 3% to ₹389 crore due to lower employee benefits and other costs.

The fund house registered a loss of ₹89 crore in other income during the quarter against a profit of ₹51 crore last year. This was driven by mark-to-market losses on investments.

Total assets under management grew 25% to ₹11.78 lakh crore from ₹9.43 lakh crore a year ago. Equity assets rose to ₹6.20 lakh crore while passive assets nearly doubled to ₹1.84 lakh crore.

SIP inflows in the March quarter jumped 31% to ₹5,104 crore from ₹3,906 crore in the same period last year. The company commands 14% equity market share and 27% hybrid market share.

For the full year FY26, profit after tax stood at ₹3,298 crore compared to ₹2,651 crore in FY25. Total income for the year rose to approximately ₹6,000 crore.

"Investors increased their equity exposure when markets fell last month, and this trend has continued despite volatility in markets over the last 18 months," said Nimesh Shah, Managing Director & CEO, ICICI Prudential AMC.

The board recommended a final dividend of ₹12.40 per equity share for FY26. The payout is subject to approval at the upcoming annual general meeting.

Shares of ICICI Prudential AMC settled 1.14% lower at ₹3,351.60 on the BSE ahead of the results announcement. The stock has gained about 29% year-to-date.
