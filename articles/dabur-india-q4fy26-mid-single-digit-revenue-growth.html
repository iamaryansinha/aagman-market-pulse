Dabur India Expects Mid-Single Digit Revenue Growth in Q4 FY26

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Dabur India expects mid-single digit revenue growth for Q4 FY26 ending March 31, 2026
  • 02/Strong domestic demand in India helped offset challenges in West Asia markets
  • 03/Geopolitical tensions in West Asia disrupted demand and supply chains in the region

Dabur India Ltd expects to report mid-single digit revenue growth in Q4 FY26. The FMCG major provided the guidance ahead of its quarterly results.

Strong domestic demand helped drive volume growth in the Indian market. The company's core Ayurvedic healthcare and personal care segments showed resilience.

However, West Asia operations faced significant headwinds. Geopolitical tensions in the region disrupted both demand patterns and supply chain logistics.

Dabur derives approximately 10-12% of its revenue from international markets. West Asia is a key region for its hair oils and healthcare products.

The company has been expanding its rural distribution network. It added over 50,000 new retail touchpoints during the fiscal year.

Input cost pressures have moderated compared to the previous year. This has provided some margin relief to the FMCG sector.

Dabur shares closed at ₹587.40 on April 3, 2026. The stock has gained 8% over the past year.

DABUR INDIA EXPECTS MID-SINGLE DIGIT REVENUE GROWTH FOR THE QUARTER ENDING MARCH 31, 2026, TRANSLATING TO APPROXIMATELY 4-6% YEAR-OVER-YEAR GROWTH.
WHAT DROVE GROWTH FOR DABUR IN Q4?
STRONG DOMESTIC DEMAND IN THE INDIAN MARKET DROVE VOLUME GROWTH, WITH CORE AYURVEDIC HEALTHCARE AND PERSONAL CARE SEGMENTS SHOWING RESILIENCE DESPITE CHALLENGING CONDITIONS.
WHAT CHALLENGES DID DABUR FACE IN INTERNATIONAL MARKETS?
GEOPOLITICAL TENSIONS IN WEST ASIA DISRUPTED BOTH DEMAND PATTERNS AND SUPPLY CHAIN LOGISTICS IN THE REGION, WHICH ACCOUNTS FOR A SIGNIFICANT PORTION OF DABUR'S INTERNATIONAL REVENUE.
HOW MUCH OF DABUR'S REVENUE COMES FROM INTERNATIONAL MARKETS?
DABUR DERIVES APPROXIMATELY 10-12% OF ITS TOTAL REVENUE FROM INTERNATIONAL MARKETS, WITH WEST ASIA BEING A KEY REGION FOR HAIR OILS AND HEALTHCARE PRODUCTS.
WHAT DISTRIBUTION EXPANSION HAS DABUR UNDERTAKEN?
DABUR ADDED OVER 50,000 NEW RETAIL TOUCHPOINTS IN RURAL INDIA DURING FY26, EXPANDING ITS REACH AND DRIVING VOLUME GROWTH IN NON-URBAN MARKETS.
