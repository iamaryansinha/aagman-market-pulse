FundsIndia Crosses ₹25,000 Crore AUM Milestone, Records 58% YoY Growth

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/FundsIndia crosses ₹25,000 crore AUM milestone on March 26, 2026, recording 58% year-on-year growth and a three-year CAGR of 38%
  • 02/The Chennai-based platform serves 30 lakh users with retail investors accounting for 77% of total assets and HNI/UHNI contributing 23%
  • 03/FundsIndia competes with Zerodha Coin and Groww in India's ₹80 lakh crore mutual fund industry driven by rising SIP participation

Chennai-based digital wealth platform FundsIndia has crossed ₹25,000 crore in Assets Under Management. The milestone was announced on March 26, 2026, marking a 58% year-on-year growth.

The platform recorded net inflows of ₹2,100 crore in the recent period. Cumulative inflows over the last 24 months stand at ₹2,900 crore.

FundsIndia now serves 30 lakh users across India. Retail investors account for 77% of total assets, while HNI and UHNI investors contribute 23%.

The company operates on a three-year CAGR of 38%. The growth has been driven by sustained retail participation and stronger engagement within its IFA partner ecosystem.

SIP book stood at ₹142 crore with an incremental monthly run-rate of ₹2 crore. Mutual funds remain the largest contributor, complemented by PMS, AIF offerings, fixed-income products, and stocks.

Geographically, 39% of investors come from T30 cities. The remaining 61% are from B30 markets, indicating strong penetration beyond metros.

"Crossing ₹25,000 crore in AUM marks another significant milestone in our journey," said Akshay Sapru, Group CEO at FundsIndia. "Our strength lies in our focused verticals that create a powerful, integrated ecosystem."

FundsIndia was founded in 2009 by C.R. Chandrasekar and Srikanth Meenakshi. It pioneered online mutual fund distribution in India before newer entrants like Zerodha Coin and Groww entered the market.

The milestone comes as India's mutual fund industry AUM crossed ₹80 lakh crore in December 2025. The industry added approximately 64 lakh new investors in 2025 despite market volatility.

Monthly SIP flows in the industry stood at ₹31,000 crore in December 2025. Individual investors' AUM has grown nearly 8x over the past decade, outpacing the industry's 6x expansion.
