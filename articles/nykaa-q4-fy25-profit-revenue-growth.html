Nykaa Q4 FY25 Profit Doubles to ₹19 Crore, Revenue Up 24%

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Nykaa's Q4 FY25 net profit surged 110% YoY to ₹19 crore, up from ₹9 crore in Q4 FY24
  • 02/Revenue from operations grew 24% YoY to ₹2,062 crore, while GMV expanded 27% to ₹4,102 crore
  • 03/Full year FY25 profit stood at ₹72 crore (up 81% YoY); EBITDA margin improved to 6.5% from 5.6%

FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd (Nykaa) (NSE: NYKAA) reported strong Q4 FY25 results with net profit doubling to ₹19 crore. The profit growth marks a 110% increase compared to ₹9 crore in the same quarter last year.

Revenue from operations rose 24% year-on-year to ₹2,062 crore in Q4 FY25. Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) grew 27% to ₹4,102 crore during the quarter.

EBITDA for the quarter expanded 43% YoY to ₹133 crore. The EBITDA margin improved to 6.5% versus 5.6% in Q4 FY24, reflecting better operational efficiency.

For the full financial year FY25, net profit stood at ₹72 crore, up 81% from ₹40 crore in FY24. Annual revenue reached ₹7,950 crore, registering 24% growth year-on-year.

The beauty vertical delivered robust performance with FY25 GMV of ₹11,775 crore, up 30% YoY. The segment contributed 92% of total Q4 revenue at ₹1,894 crore.

Nykaa's physical retail network expanded significantly, adding 50 stores in FY25 to reach 237 stores across 79 cities. This marks the company's highest annual store rollout.

The fashion segment showed recovery with Q4 GMV growth of 18% YoY after muted earlier quarters. Full year fashion GMV stood at ₹3,804 crore with 12% growth.

Superstore, Nykaa's B2B distribution arm, achieved 57% GMV growth to ₹941 crore in FY25. The platform now serves over 2,76,000 retailers across nearly 1,100 cities.

Owned brands Dot & Key and Kay Beauty collectively contributed ₹1,700 crore in GMV. Dot & Key alone crossed ₹900 crore in annual GMV.

"Nykaa maintained its robust growth trajectory in Q4 FY2025, delivering strong GMV growth and expanding profitability," the company stated in its press release.

The company added several international beauty brands in FY25 including Yves Saint Laurent, NARS, Kerastase, Armani Beauty, and Chanel Beauty.

Nykaa shares closed at ₹200.80 on the NSE, giving the company a market capitalization of approximately ₹57,406 crore.
