Digi Yatra Launched At Jolly Grant Airport Dehradun By Aviation Minister

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Union Civil Aviation Minister Ram Mohan Naidu virtually launched Digi Yatra at Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun on March 29, 2026
  • 02/Digi Yatra is now operational at over 24 airports across India with 21 million registered users and 85 million journeys facilitated
  • 03/The biometric system reduces first security checkpoint wait time from 4 minutes to 45 seconds; data auto-deletes within 24 hours

Union Civil Aviation Minister Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu launched Digi Yatra at Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun on March 29, 2026. The minister virtually inaugurated the facility from Rajkot in Gujarat.

Passengers can now enter the airport seamlessly using facial recognition technology. This eliminates the need for manual document checks at multiple touchpoints.

The Digi Yatra facility enables contactless and paperless boarding. Travelers validate their identity using facial recognition at entry gates, security checkpoints, and boarding gates.

The system uses Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) based ecosystem with Facial Recognition Technology (FRT). Passengers register once on the Digi Yatra app using Aadhaar validation and selfie capture.

ID and travel credentials are stored in a secure wallet on the passenger's smartphone. No central storage of Personally Identifiable Information (PII) exists; data is purged from servers within 24 hours of use.

Digi Yatra is now available at over 24 airports across India. The platform has 21 million app users and has facilitated more than 85 million journeys since its December 2022 launch.

Average wait time at the first security checkpoint has dropped from 4 minutes to 45 seconds. The system processes passenger entry in approximately 5 seconds compared to 15 seconds manually.

"I am travelling from Dehradun to Jaipur. We are getting a lot of convenience from check-in to security. Passengers do not need to stand in line," said Divya Singhaniya, a traveler at the airport.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS DIGI YATRA AND HOW DOES IT WORK AT JOLLY GRANT AIRPORT?
DIGI YATRA IS A BIOMETRIC BOARDING SYSTEM THAT USES FACIAL RECOGNITION TECHNOLOGY FOR CONTACTLESS AIRPORT PROCESSING. PASSENGERS REGISTER ON THE DIGI YATRA APP USING AADHAAR VALIDATION AND SELF-IMAGE CAPTURE. AT JOLLY GRANT AIRPORT, TRAVELERS CAN NOW ENTER TERMINALS, CLEAR SECURITY, AND BOARD FLIGHTS USING ONLY FACIAL RECOGNITION WITHOUT SHOWING PHYSICAL DOCUMENTS REPEATEDLY.
WHO LAUNCHED DIGI YATRA AT JOLLY GRANT AIRPORT AND WHEN?
UNION CIVIL AVIATION MINISTER KINJARAPU RAM MOHAN NAIDU LAUNCHED DIGI YATRA AT JOLLY GRANT AIRPORT IN DEHRADUN ON MARCH 29, 2026. THE MINISTER VIRTUALLY INAUGURATED THE FACILITY FROM RAJKOT IN GUJARAT.
HOW MANY AIRPORTS IN INDIA HAVE DIGI YATRA FACILITY?
DIGI YATRA IS NOW OPERATIONAL AT OVER 24 AIRPORTS ACROSS INDIA. THE PLATFORM WAS INITIALLY LAUNCHED AT THREE AIRPORTS — DELHI, BENGALURU, AND VARANASI — IN DECEMBER 2022. EXPANSION TO 17 MORE AIRPORTS IS PLANNED IN THE CURRENT FINANCIAL YEAR.
HOW MANY PASSENGERS USE DIGI YATRA AND WHAT ARE THE TIME SAVINGS?
DIGI YATRA HAS 21 MILLION APP USERS AND HAS FACILITATED OVER 85 MILLION JOURNEYS. THE SYSTEM REDUCES WAIT TIME AT THE FIRST SECURITY CHECKPOINT FROM 4 MINUTES TO 45 SECONDS. PASSENGER ENTRY PROCESSING TIME HAS DROPPED FROM 15 SECONDS MANUALLY TO APPROXIMATELY 5 SECONDS.
IS PASSENGER DATA SAFE WITH DIGI YATRA?
YES. DIGI YATRA USES A PRIVACY-BY-DESIGN ARCHITECTURE WITH NO CENTRAL STORAGE OF PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION. PASSENGER DATA IS ENCRYPTED AND STORED ONLY ON THE USER'S SMARTPHONE. THE DATA IS SHARED WITH THE DEPARTURE AIRPORT FOR A BRIEF PERIOD AND AUTOMATICALLY PURGED FROM SERVERS WITHIN 24 HOURS OF USE.
