Zetwerk Pre-IPO: Eyes ₹500 Cr Funding, Files Draft Papers With SEBI

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Zetwerk files confidential draft papers with SEBI for a pre-IPO funding round targeting nearly ₹500 crore
  • 02/Company evolved from industrial marketplace to global contract manufacturer spanning aerospace, defence, renewables
  • 03/Pre-IPO round likely ahead of planned public listing; valuation and anchor investors yet to be finalized

Zetwerk has filed confidential draft papers with SEBI for a pre-IPO funding round. The manufacturing unicorn targets nearly ₹500 crore in fresh capital.

The company submitted the papers discreetly, a route increasingly popular among late-stage startups. This allows firms to test market appetite before formal IPO announcements.

Founded in 2018, Zetwerk began as an industrial goods marketplace. It has since transformed into a full-scale global contract manufacturing player.

The company now operates across electronics, aerospace, defence, renewables, oil & gas, and infrastructure. This diversification reduces dependence on any single sector.

Zetwerk's pivot positions it to capture India's manufacturing push under the PLI schemes. Defence and aerospace segments have shown strong order inflows recently.

The pre-IPO round could value the company significantly higher than its last private valuation. Existing backers include Lightspeed, Greenoaks, and SoftBank Vision Fund.
