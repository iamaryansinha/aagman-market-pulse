JFE Invests ₹7,875 Cr in JSW Kalinga, Acquires 25% Stake

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/JFE Steel invests ₹7,875 crore to acquire 25% stake in JSW Kalinga joint venture
  • 02/JSW Steel allots 25% stake to JFE following the investment agreement
  • 03/Deal strengthens JSW-JFE partnership in Indian steel market

JFE Steel has invested ₹7,875 crore in JSW Kalinga, a joint venture company of JSW Steel. The investment was announced on March 30, 2026.

Following the capital infusion, JSW Steel has allotted 25% of JSW Kalinga to JFE. This marks a significant expansion of the Japanese-Indian steel partnership.

JSW Kalinga is a key subsidiary in the JSW Steel portfolio. The company operates strategic steel assets in eastern India.

JFE is one of Japan's leading steel manufacturers. The partnership brings advanced technology and global expertise to JSW's operations.

The collaboration supports India's growing infrastructure and manufacturing demands. It also aligns with JFE's strategy to expand its presence in key Asian markets.
