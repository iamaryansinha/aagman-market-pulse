Cupid Q4 Results: Strongest Quarterly Performance, FY26 Guidance Beaten

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Cupid Ltd reports its strongest quarterly performance, set to beat FY26 guidance of ₹335 crore revenue and ₹100 crore net profit
  • 02/FY27 target set at ₹600 crore revenue with net profit margin exceeding 30% supported by export growth
  • 03/Stock gained 5% to ₹87 on Wednesday; raw material inventory secured for next six months

Cupid Ltd has announced its strongest quarterly performance to date. The condom and FMCG manufacturer is set to comfortably surpass its FY26 annual guidance.

The company guided for ₹335 crore in revenue and ₹100 crore in net profit for FY26. Strong execution and improving operating leverage have driven results above targets.

For FY27, Cupid has set an ambitious revenue target of ₹600 crore. The company expects net profit margins to exceed 30%.

Export momentum remains a key growth driver. Steady USD-INR tailwinds will support export realizations in coming quarters.

Raw material inventory is adequately secured for six months. This ensures no near-term challenges from crude-derived input costs.

Shares of Cupid Ltd gained 5% to close at ₹87 on Wednesday. The stock has attracted buying interest following the strong operational update.

Aditya Kumar Halwasiya, Chairman & Managing Director, said the record-breaking performance reflects business model strength and disciplined execution. He added that growing global demand continues to support expansion.
