Coforge Encora Acquisition Cleared: All Approvals Secured, Merger by April End

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Coforge secures all regulatory approvals for Encora acquisition across multiple jurisdictions; merger expected by April 30, 2026
  • 02/Combined entity will operate at $2.5 billion annualised revenue with $2 billion AI-led engineering, data, and cloud services core
  • 03/Stock trades at ₹1,220.00 with 26.5% YTD decline; 20-25% G&A cost savings targeted post-merger

Coforge Limited has secured all regulatory approvals for its Encora acquisition. The merger is expected to close by month-end.

CEO Sudhir Singh confirmed every element of anticipated synergies remains on course. The deal was first announced on December 26, 2025.

The combined entity will generate approximately $2.5 billion in annualised run rate revenue. The core business will focus on AI-led engineering, data, and cloud services worth $2 billion.

All senior leaders approached to stay post-merger have agreed. Integration planning is proceeding on schedule.

A cost optimisation programme will target 20-25% reduction in General & Administrative expenses. Commercial and sales teams are ready to begin collaborative operations immediately after closing.

Coforge shares traded at ₹1,220.00 on Monday, down 0.35 per cent. The stock opened at ₹1,212.30 and touched a high of ₹1,222.70.

Total market capitalisation stood at ₹40,888 crore. The stock has gained 11.7 per cent over the past month but remains down 26.5 per cent year-to-date.

The 52-week range spans ₹1,008.10 to ₹1,994.00. Annualised volatility currently stands at 43.18 per cent. The stock trades at a price-to-earnings ratio of 31.28.

"Every element of anticipated synergies remains on course" - Sudhir Singh, CEO, Coforge Limited.
