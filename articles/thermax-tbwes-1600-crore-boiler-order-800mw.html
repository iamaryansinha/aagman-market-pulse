Thermax TBWES Bags ₹1,600 Crore Boiler Package Order for 800 MW Power Plant

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/TBWES secures ₹1,600 crore boiler package order for 1x800 MW ultra-supercritical thermal power plant in Central India
  • 02/Scope includes manufacturing, supply, commissioning and performance testing of complete boiler package
  • 03/Order strengthens Thermax's position in thermal power equipment market amid growing energy demand

Thermax Babcock & Wilcox Energy Solutions (TBWES) has secured a major order worth ₹1,600 crore. The contract is from a leading thermal power projects company based in Central India.

The order covers a 1x800 MW ultra-supercritical thermal power plant. TBWES will handle manufacturing, supply, commissioning and performance testing of the boiler package.

TBWES is a joint venture between Thermax and Babcock & Wilcox. It specializes in steam generation equipment using solid, liquid and gaseous fuels.

The company also provides heat recovery systems from turbine and engine exhaust. Waste heat recovery solutions for industrial processes are part of its portfolio.

Beyond power generation, TBWES offers heaters for chemical, petrochemical and refinery segments. This diversification helps balance exposure to the cyclical power sector.

The services arm provides renovation and modernisation for aging boilers and heaters. This recurring revenue stream adds stability to the business model.

The ₹1,600 crore order represents a significant win for Thermax. It reinforces the company's capabilities in large-scale thermal power projects.
