Stocks In Focus April 13: L&T, NTPC, HUDCO, NBCC, Apollo Hospitals, AstraZeneca In News

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 2 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/L&T Realty acquires 100% stake in International Green Scapes for ₹1,123 crore in all-cash deal
  • 02/NTPC subsidiary THDC India commissions final 250 MW unit of 1,000 MW Tehri Pumped Storage Plant
  • 03/HUDCO and NBCC sign twin MoUs for redevelopment of 18,830 sq. mt. Delhi land parcel and sustainable projects

Over two dozen stocks will remain in focus on Monday, April 13, 2026. Key developments span infrastructure, healthcare, and real estate sectors.

Larsen & Toubro Realty Properties has acquired 100% stake in International Green Scapes Ltd for ₹1,123 crore. The all-cash deal gives L&T complete ownership of 58.23 lakh shares and development rights in Gurugram.

NTPC Ltd announced commercial operation of the fourth and final 250 MW unit at Tehri Pumped Storage Plant. The entire 1,000 MW project is now fully operational from April 12, 2026.

Housing and Urban Development Corporation Ltd (HUDCO) and NBCC (India) Ltd signed two strategic MoUs. The partnership covers redevelopment of 18,830 sq. mt. leasehold plot at Bhikaji Cama Place, Delhi.

AstraZeneca Pharma India Ltd plans to sell its 64-acre North Bengaluru manufacturing facility. The deal is expected to generate ₹3,400 crore for the company.

Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Ltd acquired remaining minority stake in Apollo Health and Lifestyle Ltd. The company purchased 775,744 equity shares at ₹241 each, totaling ₹18.69 crore.

Shalby Ltd received regulatory approval for kidney transplants at its Krishna Unit in Ahmedabad. The license is valid for five years from April 9, 2026.

TGV Sraac Ltd announced expanded production capacities for Potassium Hydroxide and Chloromethanes. The company aims to capture larger market share in industrial chemicals.

Enviro Infra Engineers Ltd secured Letters of Empanelment worth ₹972.19 crore for sewage treatment projects under Swachh Bharat Mission 2.0.

Zaggle Prepaid Ocean Services Ltd entered into an agreement with Generali Central Insurance Company. Zaggle will provide its Zoyer Platform services to the insurer.

Advance Agrolife Ltd received Letter of Intent from National Fertilizers Limited for agrochemical supply contract valued at ₹30.38 crore.

Everest Industries completed Phase I land divestment in Tamil Nadu for ₹100.83 crore. Total consideration including upcoming Phase II stands at ₹133.86 crore.

Panacea Biotec Ltd received ₹20.79 crore vaccine supply order from Central Medical Services Society. Delivery spans September 2026 to December 2028.
