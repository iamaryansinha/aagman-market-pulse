Nifty Crosses 24,000 Milestone; IT Stocks Bleed As Broader Market Rallies

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Nifty 50 index crossed the historic 24,000 mark for the first time in early trade on April 10, 2026
  • 02/IT sector underperformed sharply with TCS, Infosys, Wipro trading in the red while broader market rallied
  • 03/Midcap and smallcap indices outperformed, indicating strong participation beyond large-cap blue chips

The Nifty 50 index crossed the historic 24,000 mark in early trade on April 10, 2026. The milestone reflects continued buying momentum across Indian equities.

Benchmark indices opened higher tracking positive global cues. The Sensex also scaled fresh peaks in sync with the Nifty's record run.

However, IT stocks emerged as the biggest drag on the index. Heavyweights TCS, Infosys, and Wipro traded with significant cuts throughout the session.

The IT sector's underperformance contrasts sharply with the broader market rally. Midcap and smallcap indices posted stronger gains than their large-cap peers.

Banking and financial services stocks provided support to the benchmark. Select auto and consumption names also contributed to the upward momentum.

Analysts attribute the IT weakness to profit-booking after recent gains. Global recession fears and potential US tariff impacts have weighed on sector sentiment.

The market breadth remained positive with advancing stocks outpacing decliners. This indicates healthy participation across sectors beyond the headline index movers.
