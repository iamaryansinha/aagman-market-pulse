Hindustan Zinc Q4 Output Rises 2% to 3,15,000 Tonnes; Silver Production Falls

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Hindustan Zinc mined metal production rises 2% YoY to 3,15,000 tonnes in Q4 FY26
  • 02/Refined metal output increases 5% to 2,82,000 tonnes in January-March quarter
  • 03/Silver production declines compared to year-ago period; full year data awaited

Hindustan Zinc Ltd reported improved production figures for Q4 FY26. Mined metal output reached 3,15,000 tonnes.

The quarterly production marks a 2% increase year-on-year. The Vedanta Group company maintained steady operational performance.

Refined metal output showed stronger growth at 5%. Production touched 2,82,000 tonnes in the January-March quarter.

Silver production, however, declined during the period. The company did not specify the exact silver output figures.

Hindustan Zinc is India's largest zinc producer. It accounts for about 80% of domestic zinc production capacity.

Zinc prices have remained volatile amid global supply concerns. The metal is key for galvanizing steel across industries.

Full-year FY26 production data is expected shortly. Investors will watch for guidance on FY27 output targets.
