Rentomojo SEBI IPO Filing 2026: Company Plans ₹150-Crore Fresh Issue

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Rentomojo files draft red herring prospectus with SEBI to launch its initial public offering
  • 02/The company plans a fresh issue of ₹150 crore with no offer-for-sale component
  • 03/IPO proceeds will fund debt repayment, warehouse leases, experience stores, and general corporate purposes

Rentomojo has filed draft papers with SEBI for its initial public offering. The company plans a fresh issue of ₹150 crore.

There is no offer-for-sale component in the IPO. The entire amount will be raised through fresh equity issuance.

The company intends to use the proceeds for multiple purposes. These include repayment of debt obligations.

Funds will also cover lease rentals and licence fees for warehouses. This supports the company's logistics infrastructure expansion.

Additionally, proceeds will finance experience stores. These physical touchpoints help customers evaluate products before subscribing.

A portion is allocated for general corporate purposes. This provides flexibility for strategic initiatives and working capital needs.

Rentomojo operates in the furniture and appliance rental segment. The IPO marks a significant milestone in its growth journey.

The DRHP filing indicates the company's intent to tap public markets. It reflects confidence in the rental economy's long-term potential.

THE PROCEEDS WILL BE USED FOR PAYMENT OF DEBT, LEASE RENTALS AND LICENCE FEES FOR WAREHOUSES, EXPERIENCE STORES, AND GENERAL CORPORATE PURPOSES.

YES, RENTOMOJO HAS FILED DRAFT RED HERRING PROSPECTUS PAPERS WITH THE SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA FOR ITS INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING.
