Pune FPC Ships 20 Tonnes of Bananas to Dubai Under SMART Project

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Agrivartan Agro Farmers Producer Company Ltd, based in Indapur, Pune district, has successfully exported a 20-tonne consignment of bananas to Dubai
  • 02/The shipment marks a significant milestone for the farmer-producer company, with full payment realized and ambitious plans to scale up to 100 containers this season from 100 acres of export-quality banana cultivation
  • 03/The export was executed under the Hon Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) project

Maharashtra's farmer-producer company ecosystem scores another export win as Agrivartan Agro Farmers Producer Company Ltd ships 20 tonnes of bananas to Dubai, backed by the state government's SMART project.

The Indapur-based FPC executed the shipment in partnership with Pure Planet India Pvt Ltd, with Palladium Consulting India Pvt Ltd providing transaction facilitation and technical support. The collaboration ensured seamless communication and process management throughout the export cycle, according to a media statement.

Khanderao Yashwant Sonawale, Director of Agrivartan FPC, highlighted the transformative role of the SMART project in enabling market access for smallholder farmers. "Palladium Group, through the Government of Maharashtra's SMART Project, has played a key role in enabling banana exports by supporting FPCs with market linkages, guidance, and technical assistance," Sonawale stated.

The FPC has prepared 100 acres of export-quality bananas this season, with a target of shipping at least 100 containers. The successful dispatch and full payment realization underscore the viability of farmer-led agribusiness models when backed by structured institutional support.

The SMART project, formally known as the Hon Balasaheb Thackeray Agribusiness and Rural Transformation initiative, continues to demonstrate its effectiveness in bridging the gap between smallholder producers and international markets through technical support agencies like Palladium Consulting.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS AGRIVARTAN AGRO FARMERS PRODUCER COMPANY LTD?
A FARMER-PRODUCER COMPANY (FPC) BASED IN INDAPUR, PUNE DISTRICT, MAHARASHTRA, FOCUSED ON BANANA CULTIVATION AND EXPORTS.
WHAT IS THE SMART PROJECT?
THE HON BALASAHEB THACKERAY AGRIBUSINESS AND RURAL TRANSFORMATION (SMART) PROJECT IS A MAHARASHTRA GOVERNMENT INITIATIVE THAT PROVIDES MARKET LINKAGES, TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE, AND GUIDANCE TO FARMER-PRODUCER COMPANIES FOR ACCESSING DOMESTIC AND INTERNATIONAL MARKETS.
HOW MUCH BANANA PRODUCE WAS EXPORTED?
A 20-TONNE CONSIGNMENT WAS SHIPPED TO DUBAI, WITH THE FPC TARGETING AT LEAST 100 CONTAINERS THIS SEASON FROM 100 ACRES OF EXPORT-QUALITY BANANAS.
WHO FACILITATED THE EXPORT TRANSACTION?
THE EXPORT WAS EXECUTED IN PARTNERSHIP WITH PURE PLANET INDIA PVT LTD, WITH PALLADIUM CONSULTING INDIA PVT LTD PROVIDING TRANSACTION FACILITATION AND TECHNICAL SUPPORT.
WHAT DOES THIS MILESTONE INDICATE FOR MAHARASHTRA'S AGRI-SECTOR?
THE SUCCESSFUL EXPORT WITH FULL PAYMENT REALIZATION REFLECTS THE STRENGTHENING OF FARMER-LED AGRIBUSINESS MODELS IN THE STATE AND DEMONSTRATES HOW TECHNICAL SUPPORT AGENCIES CAN ENABLE LAST-MILE MARKET ACCESS, HELPING FPCS NAVIGATE BUYER NETWORKS AND COMPLY WITH EXPORT REQUIREMENTS.
