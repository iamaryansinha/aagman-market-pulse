TVS Overtakes Ola Electric To Lead India's EV Two-Wheeler Market In FY26

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/TVS Motor has overtaken Ola Electric to become the largest EV two-wheeler manufacturer in India for FY26
  • 02/The company crossed the 3-lakh annual sales milestone with nearly 39% year-on-year growth
  • 03/TVS becomes only the second company after Ola Electric to achieve 3 lakh annual EV sales

TVS Motor Company Ltd (NSE: TVSMOTOR) has dethroned Ola Electric to become India's largest electric two-wheeler manufacturer in FY26. The company crossed the 3-lakh annual sales milestone during the fiscal year.

The Chennai-based automaker reported nearly 39% year-on-year growth in EV sales. This marks a significant shift in the competitive landscape of India's electric mobility sector.

TVS becomes only the second company after Ola Electric to achieve the 3-lakh annual EV sales mark. Ola had previously dominated the segment since its market entry.

The company's iQube electric scooter has emerged as a strong challenger to Ola's S1 series. Consistent product upgrades and expanding dealership network have driven this growth.

Bajaj Auto and Ather Energy follow TVS and Ola in the EV two-wheeler rankings. The market remains highly competitive with multiple players vying for share.

TVS has invested heavily in expanding its EV manufacturing capacity. The company plans to launch multiple new electric models across segments in the coming quarters.

The EV two-wheeler segment has seen rapid adoption despite subsidy reductions under FAME II. Rising fuel costs and improving charging infrastructure continue to drive consumer interest.
