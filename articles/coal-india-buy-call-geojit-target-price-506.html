Coal India Share Price Target: Geojit Upgrades to Buy, Sees 12% Upside to ₹506

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Geojit Retail Research upgrades Coal India to Buy with target price of ₹506, implying 12% upside from CMP of ₹450.25
  • 02/Q3FY26 PAT declined 15.6% YoY to ₹7,166 crore on lower volumes; revenue fell 5.2% to ₹34,924 crore
  • 03/Broker expects recovery driven by summer power demand spike and higher e-auction realizations from geopolitical tensions

Geojit Retail Equity Research has upgraded Coal India to Buy. The broker has set a target price of ₹506, valuing the stock at 6.3x FY28E EV/EBITDA.

The stock currently trades at ₹450.25. This implies an upside potential of approximately 12% from current levels.

Coal India reported Q3FY26 results on March 30, 2026. Consolidated revenue declined 5.2% year-on-year to ₹34,924 crore.

The revenue drop was driven by lower coal off-take volumes. Softer e-auction realizations also impacted the topline.

Profit After Tax fell 15.6% to ₹7,166 crore. The decline was attributed to lower revenue and a one-time provision for executive pay scale upgradation.

Coal India produced 781 million tonne in FY25. The company aims to reach 1 billion tonne production by FY28-29.

Geojit expects an intense early Summer to accelerate power demand. This should drive a sharp recovery in coal offtake volumes.

Geopolitical tensions in Iran have pushed global energy prices higher. Industries are pivoting from costly imports to domestic coal.

This shift positions Coal India to benefit from higher e-auction realizations. The broker believes this will offset the subdued performance seen earlier in FY26.

Continued investments in clean energy projects and mineral diversification are underway. These initiatives support medium-term sustainability for the company.
