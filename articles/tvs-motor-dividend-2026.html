TVS Motor Dividend 2026: Board Approves ₹12 Per Share Payout

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/ TVS Motor Board approves interim dividend of ₹12 per share for FY2026, representing 1200% on face value.
  • 02/ Total payout amounts to ₹570 crore, reflecting strong cash generation and shareholder-friendly capital allocation.
  • 03/ Record date fixed at March 31, 2026; dividend to be credited within prescribed timelines.

TVS Motor Company has announced a significant interim dividend for its shareholders for the financial year ending March 31, 2026. In a board meeting held on March 24, 2026, the company's directors approved a substantial payout, reflecting its strong financial health and commitment to shareholder returns.

The board has declared an interim dividend of ₹12 per equity share on the face value of Re 1 per share. This translates to a 1200% dividend, resulting in a total payout of approximately ₹570 crore. The decision underscores TVS Motor's robust cash flow generation and disciplined capital allocation strategy.

"The board is pleased to recommend this interim dividend, which reflects our consistent performance and commitment to rewarding shareholders. The company continues to maintain a strong balance sheet while investing in future growth opportunities."

The record date for determining the eligibility of shareholders for the interim dividend has been fixed as Tuesday, March 31, 2026. Shareholders holding TVS Motor shares in their demat accounts as of this date will be entitled to receive the dividend.

TVS Motor's stock has shown positive momentum ahead of the board meeting, trading at ₹3,481.65 with a gain of 2.07% on the NSE. The dividend yield at current price levels works out to approximately 0.34% for this interim payout.
