India Fertilizer Stock Hits 18 MT for Kharif 2026, Up 22% YoY

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/India holds over 18 million tonnes of fertilizer stock as of March 2026, up 22% from 14.7 MT in year-ago period
  • 02/Government expects to meet 39 million tonnes of fertilizer demand for upcoming kharif 2026 season
  • 03/Higher buffer stock ensures adequate supply for farmers during peak sowing period

India has built up fertilizer stocks of over 18 million tonnes ahead of the kharif 2026 season. This compares to 14.7 million tonnes during the same period last year.

The government expects to meet approximately 39 million tonnes of fertilizer demand for kharif 2026. Kharif crops are sown with the onset of monsoon rains.

The 22% year-on-year increase in stock levels provides a stronger buffer. It reduces supply risk during the critical planting window.

Adequate fertilizer availability supports farm productivity during the kharif season. This period accounts for a significant portion of India's annual agricultural output.

The higher stock position comes amid global fertilizer market volatility. Supply chain disruptions have affected input costs worldwide.

Government procurement strategies appear to have built sufficient inventory. This should limit price shocks for farmers during peak demand.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS THE CURRENT FERTILIZER STOCK POSITION IN INDIA?
INDIA HOLDS OVER 18 MILLION TONNES OF FERTILIZER STOCK AS OF MARCH 2026. THIS REPRESENTS A 22% INCREASE COMPARED TO 14.7 MILLION TONNES IN THE YEAR-AGO PERIOD.
WHAT IS THE EXPECTED FERTILIZER DEMAND FOR KHARIF 2026?
THE GOVERNMENT EXPECTS TO MEET APPROXIMATELY 39 MILLION TONNES OF FERTILIZER DEMAND FOR THE KHARIF 2026 SEASON.
WHAT IS THE KHARIF SEASON?
KHARIF IS THE MONSOON CROPPING SEASON IN INDIA WHEN CROPS ARE SOWN IN JUNE-JULY AND HARVESTED IN OCTOBER-NOVEMBER. MAJOR KHARIF CROPS INCLUDE RICE, COTTON, AND SOYBEAN.
WHY IS THE HIGHER FERTILIZER STOCK IMPORTANT?
THE HIGHER BUFFER STOCK ENSURES ADEQUATE SUPPLY FOR FARMERS DURING THE PEAK SOWING PERIOD. IT REDUCES SUPPLY DISRUPTION RISK AND HELPS STABILIZE INPUT COSTS AMID GLOBAL MARKET VOLATILITY.
WHAT DOES THIS MEAN FOR FARMERS?
FARMERS CAN EXPECT IMPROVED FERTILIZER AVAILABILITY DURING THE UPCOMING KHARIF SEASON. THE GOVERNMENT'S PROACTIVE STOCKING SHOULD HELP MEET DEMAND WITHOUT MAJOR SUPPLY SHORTAGES.
