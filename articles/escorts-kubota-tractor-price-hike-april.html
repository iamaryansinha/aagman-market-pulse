Escorts Kubota to Hike Tractor Prices From April 15; Q4 Sales Show Growth

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Escorts Kubota announces tractor price increase effective April 15, 2026
  • 02/Price hike comes amid steady demand momentum in domestic tractor segment
  • 03/Company reports sales growth in Q4 FY26; full numbers awaited

Escorts Kubota Ltd has announced a price hike. Tractor prices will increase from April 15, 2026.

The company cited steady demand momentum as the context. Input cost pressures have been building across the industry.

Domestic tractor segment demand remains resilient. Rural cash flows have improved on better crop realizations.

Q4 FY26 sales showed growth over the previous year. Exact quarterly figures will be disclosed shortly.

Escorts Kubota is a leading farm equipment manufacturer. It competes with Mahindra Tractors and TAFE in India.

The company offers tractors under Escorts, Farmtrac, and Powertrac brands. Kubota technology powers premium offerings.

Tractor industry volumes have normalized post-pandemic. FY26 has seen moderate growth amid base effects.
