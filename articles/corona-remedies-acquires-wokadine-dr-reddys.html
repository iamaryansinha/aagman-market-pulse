Corona Remedies Acquires Wokadine Brand From Dr Reddy's in Strategic Deal

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Corona Remedies acquires Wokadine brand from Dr Reddy's Laboratories in strategic pharma deal
  • 02/Acquisition expected to create synergies with Corona's existing therapeutic portfolio offerings
  • 03/Deal strengthens Mumbai-based company's positioning in hospital-driven segments

Corona Remedies has acquired the Wokadine brand from Dr Reddy's Laboratories Ltd. The deal was announced on March 30, 2026.

Wokadine is an established antiseptic brand used in hospital and clinical settings. It has strong recognition across Indian healthcare facilities.

The acquisition is expected to create synergies with Corona's existing therapeutic portfolio. It enhances the company's positioning in hospital-driven segments.

Corona Remedies is a Mumbai-based pharmaceutical company. It has been expanding through strategic brand acquisitions in recent years.

Hospital segments represent a high-value market for pharma companies. This deal gives Corona access to established institutional distribution channels.

For Dr Reddy's, the divestment helps streamline its portfolio. The company can focus resources on core therapeutic areas.

The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The deal is subject to customary regulatory approvals.
