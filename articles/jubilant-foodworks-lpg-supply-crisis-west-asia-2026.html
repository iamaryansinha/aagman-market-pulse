Jubilant FoodWorks LPG Supply Hit by West Asia Crisis; Domino's Operations Impacted

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Jubilant FoodWorks reports LPG cylinder supply constraints affecting certain parts of its Domino's Pizza and Dunkin' Donuts store network
  • 02/Supply disruption stems from West Asia geopolitical situation causing commercial LPG distribution constraints across India
  • 03/Company is transitioning to alternate energy sources including electricity and piped natural gas to mitigate impact

Jubilant FoodWorks Ltd (NSE: JUBLFOOD) is facing LPG cylinder supply constraints. The disruption affects certain parts of its store network across India.

The company operates Domino's Pizza and Dunkin' Donuts outlets nationwide. Supply issues stem from the West Asia geopolitical situation.

Commercial LPG distribution faces constraints across the country. Oil marketing companies are struggling to maintain regular supply chains.

Jubilant FoodWorks is taking active steps to conserve LPG. The company has implemented fuel-saving measures at affected locations.

The company is working to transition to alternate energy sources. Electricity and piped natural gas (PNG) are being deployed as substitutes.

Jubilant remains in constant engagement with oil marketing companies (OMCs). Regular discussions aim to restore normal supply levels.

The operational impact remains limited according to company statements. Resilient systems are helping navigate these short-term challenges.

Jubilant FoodWorks operates over 1,800 Domino's Pizza stores in India. It is one of the largest quick-service restaurant operators in the country.
