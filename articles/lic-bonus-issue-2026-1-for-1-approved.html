LIC Bonus Issue 2026: Board Approves 1-for-1 Maiden Bonus Share Issue

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/LIC Board approves maiden bonus issue in 1-for-1 ratio on April 13, 2026
  • 02/Shareholders will receive one bonus share for every one share held
  • 03/Record date and ex-bonus date to be announced; stock may see volatile trading

Life Insurance Corporation of India Ltd (NSE: LIC) announced a historic board decision on April 13, 2026. The board approved the company's maiden bonus issue in a 1-for-1 ratio.

The decision was made during the board meeting held on Sunday evening. The news was disclosed at 9:38 PM IST through a stock exchange filing.

Shareholders will receive one bonus share for every one equity share held. This effectively doubles the number of shares held by existing investors without any additional cost.

The company has not yet announced the record date or the ex-bonus date. These dates will determine which shareholders are eligible to receive the bonus shares.

LIC is India's largest insurance company by market share and assets under management. It manages over ₹40 lakh crore in assets and serves more than 28 crore policyholders.

The bonus issue comes ahead of the company's third full financial year since listing. LIC went public in May 2022 with India's largest IPO at ₹21,000 crore.

The insurer's shares have faced pressure since listing. The stock trades significantly below its IPO price of ₹949 per share.

This bonus issue is seen as a move to reward existing shareholders. It may also improve the stock's liquidity by increasing the number of shares in circulation.

WHAT IS THE BONUS ISSUE RATIO APPROVED BY LIC BOARD?
THE LIC BOARD HAS APPROVED A 1-FOR-1 BONUS ISSUE RATIO. THIS MEANS SHAREHOLDERS WILL RECEIVE ONE BONUS SHARE FOR EVERY ONE EQUITY SHARE THEY CURRENTLY HOLD, EFFECTIVELY DOUBLING THEIR HOLDINGS.
WHEN WAS THE LIC BONUS ISSUE APPROVED?
THE LIC BOARD APPROVED THE MAIDEN BONUS ISSUE ON APRIL 13, 2026, DURING A BOARD MEETING HELD ON SUNDAY EVENING. THE ANNOUNCEMENT WAS MADE AT 9:38 PM IST THROUGH A REGULATORY FILING.
IS THIS LIC'S FIRST BONUS ISSUE?
YES, THIS IS LIC'S MAIDEN BONUS ISSUE. IT IS THE FIRST TIME THE COMPANY HAS DECLARED A BONUS ISSUE SINCE ITS INCEPTION AND SUBSEQUENT LISTING ON THE STOCK EXCHANGES IN MAY 2022.
WHEN WILL SHAREHOLDERS RECEIVE THE BONUS SHARES?
THE RECORD DATE AND EX-BONUS DATE HAVE NOT BEEN ANNOUNCED YET. SHAREHOLDERS HOLDING LIC SHARES IN THEIR DEMAT ACCOUNTS ON THE RECORD DATE WILL BE ELIGIBLE TO RECEIVE THE BONUS SHARES.
WHAT DOES A 1-FOR-1 BONUS ISSUE MEAN FOR INVESTORS?
A 1-FOR-1 BONUS ISSUE MEANS FOR EVERY ONE SHARE AN INVESTOR HOLDS, THEY WILL RECEIVE ONE ADDITIONAL SHARE FREE OF COST. IF AN INVESTOR HOLDS 100 SHARES, THEY WILL GET 100 BONUS SHARES, TAKING THE TOTAL TO 200 SHARES. THE SHARE PRICE WILL ADJUST DOWNWARD PROPORTIONATELY AFTER THE EX-BONUS DATE.
