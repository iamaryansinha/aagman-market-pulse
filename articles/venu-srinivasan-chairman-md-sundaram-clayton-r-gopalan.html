Venu Srinivasan Re-Designated as Chairman & MD of Sundaram Clayton After R Gopalan Exit

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Sundaram Clayton board re-designates Venu Srinivasan as Chairman & MD effective March 30, 2026
  • 02/R Gopalan steps down as Chairman with immediate effect; continues as Non-Executive Independent Director
  • 03/Leadership change comes amid stock decline; shares down 40% over past year from 52-week high of ₹2,538

Sundaram Clayton Limited (NSE: SUNCLAY) has announced a key leadership change. The board has re-designated Venu Srinivasan as Chairman and Managing Director of the company.

The re-designation follows R Gopalan stepping down as Chairman with immediate effect. Gopalan will continue to serve as a Non-Executive Independent Director on the board.

Srinivasan was previously holding the position of Chairman Emeritus and Managing Director. He has now been elevated to Chairman & MD following Gopalan's departure.

Sundaram Clayton is a leading manufacturer of aluminum die castings for the automotive sector. The company supplies precision components to OEMs and Tier 1 customers across India and global markets including the US, Germany, and Japan.

The leadership change comes at a challenging time for the stock. Sundaram Clayton shares closed at ₹1,283.20 on March 27, 2026. The stock has declined approximately 40% over the past year from its 52-week high of ₹2,538.10.

The company operates five manufacturing plants with capabilities in high-pressure, low-pressure, and gravity die-casting. It recently commenced operations at its new Thervoy Kandigai facility in Tiruvallur.

Venu Srinivasan is a veteran industrialist who joined Sundaram-Clayton as Managing Director in 1979. Under his leadership, the company won the prestigious Deming Prize for quality management.
