United Spirits Signs Virat Kohli as Johnnie Walker Luxe Brand Ambassador

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/United Spirits appoints Virat Kohli as brand ambassador for Johnnie Walker Luxe Blended Water
  • 02/Move aligns with USL's strategy to strengthen premium and luxury positioning in India's alcobev market
  • 03/Campaign launches as competition intensifies in India's growing premium beverages segment

United Spirits Limited has appointed Virat Kohli as the brand ambassador for Johnnie Walker Luxe Blended Water.

The announcement marks a significant push into the premium non-alcoholic beverage segment. Kohli will front the brand's new campaign.

USL continues to invest heavily in premium brand positioning. The strategy targets India's competitive alcobev and luxury beverage market.

Johnnie Walker Luxe Blended Water is positioned as a premium offering. It complements USL's broader portfolio of luxury spirits.

The partnership with Kohli signals USL's intent to capture the aspirational consumer segment. Celebrity endorsements remain a key marketing lever in India.

The campaign comes as rival beverage makers intensify their premiumization efforts. India's luxury beverage market has shown resilient growth despite economic headwinds.
