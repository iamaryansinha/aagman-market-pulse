20 Stocks to Watch Monday: MCX, BSE, JSW Steel, Oberoi Realty Lead List

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/MCX (NSE: MCX) in focus as NSE launches Dated Brent Crude Oil futures, intensifying competition in commodity derivatives
  • 02/TVS Holdings, BSE, JSW Steel, Oberoi Realty, RailTel, Gujarat Inject, and PC Jeweller among 20 stocks on watchlist
  • 03/Multiple companies announce acquisitions, orders, and leadership changes ahead of Monday trading session

Twenty stocks are on traders' radar for Monday's session. Key names include MCX, BSE, JSW Steel, and Oberoi Realty.

MCX India (NSE: MCX) faces fresh competition. NSE has launched Dated Brent Crude Oil futures contracts, directly challenging MCX's dominance in energy derivatives.

The move could impact MCX's trading volumes. Brent crude is among the most actively traded commodity contracts in India.

BSE Ltd (NSE: BSE) also features on the watchlist. The exchange operator has seen heightened activity amid evolving market structure.

JSW Steel (NSE: JSWSTEEL) remains in focus. The steel giant continues to navigate raw material costs and export market dynamics.

Oberoi Realty (NSE: OBEROIRLTY) attracts attention. The Mumbai-based developer is benefiting from strong housing demand in key metros.

RailTel Corporation (NSE: RAILTEL) makes the list. The PSU has been securing digital infrastructure orders across railway networks.

Gujarat Inject and PC Jeweller round out notable mentions. Both stocks often see speculative interest during volatile market phases.

Traders should monitor volumes closely. Corporate announcements over the weekend could spark gap-up or gap-down openings.
