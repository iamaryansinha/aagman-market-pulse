Intellius Recode Files DRHP With SEBI; Plans ₹117 Crore IPO

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Intellius Recode files draft IPO papers with SEBI to raise ₹117 crore through a fresh issue of equity shares
  • 02/The IPO includes an offer-for-sale of up to 12.9 lakh equity shares by existing shareholders
  • 03/Proceeds will fund digital worker development (₹43.1 crore), sub-contracting fees (₹38.4 crore), and general corporate purposes

Intellius Recode Ltd has filed draft red herring prospectus (DRHP) with SEBI. The company plans to raise ₹117 crore through an initial public offering.

The IPO comprises a fresh issue of equity shares aggregating ₹117 crore. It also includes an offer-for-sale (OFS) of up to 12.9 lakh equity shares by existing shareholders.

Intellius Recode is a technology solutions provider based in India. It enables digital transformation for enterprise clients through AI-led solutions.

The company's flagship offering includes "Agentic AI"-based digital workers. These are AI-enabled software products designed to function as virtual employees for businesses.

The technology consulting vertical covers data analytics, robotic process automation, and integration services. It also includes development operations, quality assurance, and digital commerce solutions.

The company intends to utilise ₹43.1 crore for developing digital workers. Another ₹38.4 crore will go toward payment of sub-contracting fees related to their development.

For FY2025, Intellius Recode reported revenue of ₹70.8 crore. This represents an 11% decline from ₹79.8 crore in FY2024.

Profit after tax dropped 11.5% to ₹8.2 crore in FY2025. This compares to ₹9.2 crore in the previous fiscal year.

Inga Ventures is the book-running lead manager for the issue. The company has not raised any prior funding from external investors.
