Ola Electric Launches S1 X+ 5.2 kWh With Indigenous 4680 Bharat Cells at ₹1,29,999

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Ola Electric Mobility Ltd launches S1 X+ 5.2 kWh e-scooter on April 13, 2026, priced at ₹1,29,999 introductory rate valid till April 15
  • 02/The scooter is powered by Ola's indigenous 4680 Bharat Cells, marking a major step in local battery manufacturing
  • 03/Watch how this launch impacts monthly registrations and whether the mass-market pricing helps regain market share

Ola Electric Mobility Ltd (NSE: OLAELEC) launched the S1 X+ 5.2 kWh electric scooter on April 13, 2026. The introductory price is set at ₹1,29,999, valid until April 15, 2026.

The new variant is powered by Ola's indigenously developed 4680 Bharat Cells. This marks a significant milestone in the company's push for domestic battery production.

The S1 X+ 5.2 kWh delivers a certified range of 320 km under IDC conditions. Peak power output stands at 11 kW, with a top speed of 130 kmph.

Key features include a mid-drive motor with integrated MCU, front disc brake, and brake-by-wire technology. The scooter also gets a 4.3-inch coloured segmented LCD display.

Ola Electric has positioned this variant in the mass-premium segment. It is available in ten colour options across glossy and matte finishes.

The launch comes as the company reports a demand rebound. March 2026 registrations touched 10,117 units, up from 3,973 units in February.

Daily orders exceeded 1,000 units in the last week of March. The company also crossed 1 million cumulative registrations recently.

However, investors should note the recent weak quarterly performance. Q3 FY26 deliveries fell 61% YoY to 32,680 units, and revenue dropped 55%.

The success of the S1 X+ 5.2 kWh will depend on sustained demand beyond the introductory pricing window. Competitors like TVS Motor and Bajaj Auto continue to strengthen their electric portfolios.
