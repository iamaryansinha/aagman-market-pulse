Gold Loans Drive India's Credit Supply Growth in Q4 2025: CMI Report Shows Volume, Value Surge

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/India's Credit Market Index (CMI) for supply increased in Q4 2025, primarily driven by gold loan originations
  • 02/Gold loans saw higher growth in both volume and value terms during the December 2025 quarter
  • 03/Trend reflects rural credit demand and potential stress in informal lending channels

India's credit supply expanded in the December 2025 quarter. The Credit Market Index (CMI) for supply rose significantly.

Gold loans emerged as the primary growth driver. Both volume and value of gold loan originations increased during the period.

The surge indicates strong credit demand from rural and semi-urban borrowers. Gold-backed lending remains a preferred credit channel for households.

Non-banking financial companies (NBFCs) and banks have scaled up gold loan portfolios. The asset class offers secured exposure with lower default risk.

Rising gold prices have improved loan-to-value ratios for existing borrowers. This enables higher ticket sizes for new originations.

The data suggests liquidity constraints in informal credit markets. Borrowers are shifting from moneylenders to regulated institutions.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS THE CREDIT MARKET INDEX (CMI) AND WHAT DOES IT MEASURE?
THE CREDIT MARKET INDEX (CMI) IS AN INDICATOR THAT TRACKS THE SUPPLY AND DEMAND DYNAMICS IN INDIA'S CREDIT MARKETS. THE SUPPLY COMPONENT MEASURES THE AVAILABILITY AND ORIGINATION OF CREDIT ACROSS VARIOUS LENDING CHANNELS INCLUDING BANKS AND NBFCs.
WHICH CREDIT SEGMENT DROVE THE CMI GROWTH IN Q4 2025?
GOLD LOANS WERE THE PRIMARY DRIVER OF CMI SUPPLY GROWTH IN THE DECEMBER 2025 QUARTER. THE REPORT HIGHLIGHTED INCREASED ORIGINATIONS IN BOTH VOLUME AND VALUE TERMS FOR GOLD-BACKED LENDING.
WHY ARE GOLD LOANS GROWING IN INDIA?
GOLD LOANS ARE GROWING DUE TO STRONG RURAL CREDIT DEMAND, RISING GOLD PRICES IMPROVING COLLATERAL VALUES, AND A SHIFT FROM INFORMAL MONEY LENDERS TO REGULATED LENDING CHANNELS. THEY OFFER QUICK DISBURSAL WITH MINIMAL DOCUMENTATION.
WHICH LENDERS BENEFIT FROM GOLD LOAN GROWTH?
BOTH PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SECTOR BANKS ALONG WITH GOLD-FOCUSED NBFCs BENEFIT. MAJOR PLAYERS INCLUDE MANAPPURAM FINANCE, MUTHOOT FINANCE, AND GOLD LOAN PORTFOLIOS OF LARGE BANKS LIKE SBI AND ICICI BANK.
WHAT DOES THIS TREND INDICATE FOR THE ECONOMY?
THE TREND SIGNALS STRONG CREDIT DEMAND IN RURAL AREAS AND POTENTIAL LIQUIDITY STRESS AMONG HOUSEHOLDS. IT ALSO REFLECTS FINANCIAL DEEPENING AS BORROWERS MOVE FROM UNORGIZED TO FORMAL LENDING CHANNELS.
