IRB Infrastructure Bonus Issue 2026: Stock Jumps 9% on Ex-Date, 1:1 Ratio

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/IRB Infrastructure shares surged 9% on March 30, 2026, as stock began trading ex-bonus for 1:1 bonus issue
  • 02/Record date fixed at April 1, 2026; shareholders with 100 shares will receive 100 bonus shares
  • 03/Allotment scheduled for April 2, 2026; share price adjusts proportionately downward

IRB Infrastructure Developers Ltd shares jumped 9% on March 30, 2026. The stock began trading ex-bonus for its 1:1 bonus issue.

The company announced the bonus issue on February 13, 2026. Shareholders approved it through postal ballot on March 23, 2026.

The record date is fixed at Wednesday, April 1, 2026. Only shareholders holding shares before the ex-date are eligible.

The bonus ratio is 1:1. A shareholder with 100 shares will receive 100 additional bonus shares. Total holding becomes 200 shares post-allotment.

The share price adjusts proportionately downward after the bonus. The stock price halves to reflect the doubled share count.

Shares were trading at ₹22.28 on March 30, up 8.8% after adjusting for the bonus. The pre-adjustment price was approximately ₹44.

This marks IRB Infrastructure's first bonus issue. The company previously executed a stock split in February 2023.

The 2023 split divided one share of ₹10 face value into ten shares of ₹1 each. Current face value is Re 1 per share.

Bonus shares will be credited to eligible shareholders' demat accounts by April 2, 2026. Trading in bonus shares begins shortly after.

IRB Infrastructure is a leading roads and highways infrastructure developer in India. The company operates multiple toll road projects across the country.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS THE BONUS ISSUE RATIO FOR IRB INFRASTRUCTURE?
IRB INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPERS LTD HAS DECLARED A BONUS ISSUE IN THE RATIO OF 1:1. THIS MEANS SHAREHOLDERS WILL RECEIVE ONE BONUS SHARE FOR EVERY ONE EXISTING SHARE HELD AS ON THE RECORD DATE.
WHAT IS THE RECORD DATE FOR IRB INFRASTRUCTURE BONUS ISSUE?
THE RECORD DATE TO DETERMINE ELIGIBILITY FOR THE BONUS ISSUE IS WEDNESDAY, APRIL 1, 2026. SHAREHOLDERS MUST HOLD SHARES IN THEIR DEMAT ACCOUNTS AS OF THIS DATE TO QUALIFY.
WHEN IS THE EX-DATE FOR IRB INFRASTRUCTURE BONUS ISSUE?
THE EX-BONUS DATE IS MONDAY, MARCH 30, 2026. THIS IS ONE BUSINESS DAY BEFORE THE RECORD DATE. SHARES PURCHASED ON OR AFTER THIS DATE WILL NOT BE ELIGIBLE FOR THE BONUS.
WHEN WILL THE BONUS SHARES BE ALLOTTED?
THE DEEMED ALLOTMENT DATE FOR BONUS SHARES IS APRIL 2, 2026. ELIGIBLE SHAREHOLDERS WILL RECEIVE BONUS SHARES IN THEIR DEMAT ACCOUNTS ON OR BEFORE THIS DATE.
HOW WILL THE BONUS ISSUE AFFECT MY SHAREHOLDING AND SHARE PRICE?
IF YOU HOLD 100 SHARES AS ON THE RECORD DATE, YOU WILL RECEIVE 100 BONUS SHARES. YOUR TOTAL HOLDING BECOMES 200 SHARES. THE SHARE PRICE WILL HALF PROPORTIONATELY TO REFLECT THE INCREASED NUMBER OF SHARES OUTSTANDING.
