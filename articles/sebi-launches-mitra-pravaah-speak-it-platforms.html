SEBI Unveils Three New IT Platforms: MITRA, PRAVAAH, SPEAK For FY2026-27

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/SEBI launches three new IT platforms MITRA, PRAVAAH, and SPEAK to transform India's regulatory landscape
  • 02/Phased rollout begins FY2026-27 with collaboration from exchanges, depositories, and market participants
  • 03/Platforms aim to enable real-time surveillance, reduce compliance burden, and improve market integrity

SEBI has launched three new IT platforms to transform India's regulatory landscape. The platforms are MITRA, PRAVAAH, and SPEAK.

MITRA focuses on market monitoring and surveillance. It will enable real-time detection of market anomalies and irregular trading patterns.

PRAVAAH handles intermediary registration processes. It aims to streamline registrations and reduce the compliance burden for brokers and intermediaries.

SPEAK serves as a disclosure portal for market participants. It will enhance transparency and improve access to information for investors.

The phased rollout begins in FY2026-27. SEBI is collaborating with stock exchanges, depositories, and market participants for implementation.

These platforms will strengthen market integrity through faster anomaly detection. Investors will benefit from better information access and improved transparency.

The initiative marks a significant step toward digitizing India's securities market infrastructure. It aligns with global best practices in regulatory technology.

THE PLATFORMS AIM TO ENABLE REAL-TIME MARKET SURVEILLANCE, STREAMLINE INTERMEDIARY REGISTRATIONS, AND ENHANCE TRANSPARENCY IN THE SECURITIES MARKET.

THE PHASED ROLLOUT OF THESE PLATFORMS WILL BEGIN IN FINANCIAL YEAR 2026-27.

SEBI IS WORKING WITH STOCK EXCHANGES, DEPOSITORIES, AND MARKET PARTICIPANTS TO IMPLEMENT THESE PLATFORMS EFFECTIVELY.
