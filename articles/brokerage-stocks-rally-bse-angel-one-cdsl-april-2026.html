Brokerage Stocks Rally 8%: BSE, Angel One, CDSL Lead Capital Market Surge

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Brokerage and capital market-linked stocks rallied over 8% in early trade on April 1, 2026, led by BSE Ltd and Motilal Oswal Financial Services
  • 02/The rally defies the Securities Transaction Tax hike taking effect today, driven by RBI deferring capital market exposure norms to July 1
  • 03/Nifty Capital Market Index surged over 5% to 4,472; investors rotating into exchange and brokerage plays on supportive regulatory outlook

Brokerage and capital market-linked stocks staged a sharp rally in early Wednesday trade. Shares of BSE Ltd, Motilal Oswal Financial Services, Angel One, Groww and CDSL gained over 8%.

Motilal Oswal Financial Services led the pack with an 8.5% surge to ₹687.05. BSE Ltd jumped over 7% to ₹2,879.10, reflecting strong investor sentiment.

The rally comes despite headwinds from the Securities Transaction Tax hike taking effect today. Market participants had priced in negative impact from higher transaction costs.

The Reserve Bank of India provided relief by deferring capital market exposure norms. Implementation is now pushed to July 1, 2026, from the earlier April 1 deadline.

RBI also expanded the definition of acquisition finance to include mergers and amalgamations. This move broadens financing avenues for market participants.

Additionally, RBI capped loans against shares at ₹1 crore per individual at the system level. This aims to balance credit growth with prudent risk management.

The broader momentum lifted the Nifty Capital Market Index over 5% to 4,472. Computer Age Management Services and Indian Energy Exchange also posted notable gains.

THE RALLY WAS DRIVEN BY RBI DEFERRING CAPITAL MARKET EXPOSURE NORMS TO JULY 1, 2026, PROVIDING NEAR-TERM REGULATORY RELIEF DESPITE THE STT HIKE TAKING EFFECT.

THE NIFTY CAPITAL MARKET INDEX SURGED OVER 5% TO REACH 4,472, REFLECTING BROAD-BASED STRENGTH ACROSS BROKERAGE, EXCHANGE, AND DEPOSITORY STOCKS.
WHAT OTHER REGULATORY CHANGES DID RBI ANNOUNCE?
RBI EXPANDED THE DEFINITION OF ACQUISITION FINANCE TO INCLUDE MERGERS AND AMALGAMATIONS, AND CAPPED LOANS AGAINST SHARES AT ₹1 CRORE PER INDIVIDUAL AT THE SYSTEM LEVEL.
DOES THE STT HIKE IMPACT THESE STOCKS?
WHILE THE SECURITIES TRANSACTION TAX HIKE TOOK EFFECT ON APRIL 1, 2026, INVESTORS APPEAR TO HAVE LOOKED PAST SHORT-TERM COST CONCERNS FOCUSING ON THE SUPPORTIVE REGULATORY TIMELINE EXTENSION.
