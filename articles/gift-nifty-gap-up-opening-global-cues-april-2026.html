Gift Nifty Signals Gap-Up Opening as Global Cues Lift Market Sentiment

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 3 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Gift Nifty indicates gap-up opening for Indian markets following positive global cues overnight
  • 02/Asian markets trade higher tracking overnight gains in US equities and easing trade war concerns
  • 03/Key levels to watch: Nifty 50 support at 22,800, resistance at 23,200; Bank Nifty eyes 48,500

Gift Nifty is indicating a gap-up opening for Indian equity markets on April 10, 2026. The early signals suggest renewed buying interest at the opening bell.

Overnight, US markets closed on a positive note. The Dow Jones gained 1.1%, while the Nasdaq rose 1.3% on technology buying.

Asian markets are trading higher in morning sessions. Japan's Nikkei 225 is up 0.9%, while Hong Kong's Hang Seng gained 1.2%.

The positive sentiment follows easing concerns over trade tensions. Investors are repositioning for the new earnings season beginning next week.

Foreign institutional investors (FIIs) turned net buyers in the previous session. They purchased equities worth ₹892 crore after six consecutive days of selling.

Crude oil prices have stabilized near $72 per barrel. This provides relief for import-dependent economies like India.

The rupee opened flat at 85.42 against the US dollar. Currency traders expect range-bound movement ahead of the US inflation data.

Analysts suggest traders watch key support and resistance levels. Any breakout above 23,200 could trigger further short-covering rallies.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS //
WHAT IS GIFT NIFTY AND WHY DOES IT MATTER?
GIFT NIFTY IS A DERIVATIVES CONTRACT TRADED AT THE GIFT CITY IN GUJARAT, INDIA. IT TRACKS THE NIFTY 50 INDEX AND TRADES FOR 21 HOURS DAILY, PROVIDING EARLY INDICATIONS OF HOW INDIAN MARKETS WILL OPEN.
WHAT ARE THE KEY LEVELS TO WATCH FOR NIFTY 50 TODAY?
IMMEDIATE SUPPORT FOR NIFTY 50 IS AT 22,800, WHILE RESISTANCE IS SEEN AT 23,200. A SUSTAINED BREAKOUT ABOVE 23,200 COULD TRIGGER SHORT-COVERING AND PUSH THE INDEX TOWARD 23,500.
WHY ARE GLOBAL MARKETS TRADING HIGHER TODAY?
GLOBAL MARKETS ARE RALLYING ON EASING TRADE WAR CONCERNS, STRONG US CORPORATE EARNINGS EXPECTATIONS, AND STABILIZING COMMODITY PRICES. THE DOW JONES GAINED 1.1% AND NASDAQ ROSE 1.3% OVERNIGHT.
WHAT IS THE FII ACTIVITY INDICATING?
FOREIGN INSTITUTIONAL INVESTORS PURCHASED INDIAN EQUITIES WORTH ₹892 CRORE ON APRIL 9, 2026. THIS MARKS A REVERSAL FROM SIX CONSECUTIVE SESSIONS OF NET SELLING, SUGGESTING POTENTIAL STABILIZATION IN FLOWS.
WHAT SECTORS SHOULD INVESTORS WATCH TODAY?
INVESTORS SHOULD TRACK IT STOCKS FOLLOWING THE NASDAQ RALLY, BANKING STOCKS AHEAD OF THE RBI POLICY MEETING, AND OIL & GAS COMPANIES AS CRUDE PRICES STABILIZE NEAR $72 PER BARREL.
