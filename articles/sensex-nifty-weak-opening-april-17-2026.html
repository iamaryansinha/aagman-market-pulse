Sensex, Nifty Set for Weak Opening on April 17 Amid Mixed Global Cues

BY AAGMAN DESK | EST. READ: 1 MIN
EXECUTIVE TLDR //
  • 01/Gift Nifty trading at 24,153 indicates Indian markets will open marginally lower on Friday, April 17, 2026
  • 02/Mixed global cues driving cautious sentiment among investors ahead of the trading session
  • 03/Markets eye global developments and crude oil movement for intraday direction

Indian equity markets are poised for a cautious start on Friday, April 17, 2026. The Gift Nifty is trading at 24,153, signaling a marginal decline at the opening bell.

Benchmark indices Sensex and Nifty are expected to open weak. Traders are adopting a wait-and-watch approach amid mixed global signals.

Asian markets are presenting a mixed picture. US markets overnight showed volatile movement, contributing to the uncertain sentiment.

Crude oil prices have softened with Brent declining to $98.05 per barrel. This could provide some relief to oil-importing sectors.

The Nifty has been consolidating in a range over recent sessions. Support is seen around 24,000 while resistance hovers near 24,300.

Investors are advised to watch global cues closely. Volatility may persist through the trading session.
